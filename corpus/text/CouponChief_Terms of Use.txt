1.
Program Overview.
The CouponChief Pays-2-Share program allows CouponChief.com Users to add coupons for participating merchants, and earn money when other Users of the site subsequently use those coupons.
The Program is free to Users, and is conferred as a benefit of registration with CouponChief Pays-2-Share program.
You are under no obligation to participate or redeem your Pays-2-Share money.
As a User, CouponChief Pays-2-Share money is automatically earned when someone who uses your coupons makes a qualifying online purchase from a merchant participating in Pays-2-Share on our Stores page.
Some Participating Merchants may have special requirements to earn Pays-2-Share money.
Further, not all merchants listed on our Stores page participate in the Program.
Participating Merchant's are identified by a yellow cent icon.
2.
Eligibility.
The Program is offered for purchases made through links on the CouponChief.com website.
You must have Internet access and a valid working e-mail address to receive the privileges of the Program.
Further, participation in the Program is limited to U.S.
residents that are 18 years of age or older.
Only one Pays-2-Share account is allowed per household.
3.
Earning CouponChief Pays-2-Share money.
To be eligible to receive your funds, other Users of the site must make a qualifying purchase.
Generally, in order to make a qualifying purchase, one must satisfy the following criteria:
 (1).
One must be 18 years of age or older. (2).
One must not be a direct visitor from a paid-advertising campaign, including, but not limited to: pay-per-click ads, banner advertising, and sub-affiliate relationships; (3).
One must link to a Participating Merchant from CouponChief's Stores/Offers page; (4).
One must add items to their cart; (5).
One must complete your transaction.
and (6).
One must have cookies enabled on your web browser (7).
One must reside in the U.S.A.
or Canada 
Pays-2-Share User's earnings are calculated based on 2% of qualifying purchases, minus taxes, returns, adjustments, and order cancelations.
Further, each User's earnings are limited to $25 per participating store, per month.
A single coupon is eligible for commissions for up to 60 days after the date is becomes live, or when the offer expires, whichever comes first.
There is no limit on the total amount a Pays-2-Share User may earn per month.
Reasons why you will not earn Pays-2-Share money for another Users purchase, include, but are not limited to, the following:

You go directly to the participating merchant without first visiting our Stores page; (3).
You complete multiple transactions without returning to our Stores page.
(Completing each transaction ends their purchase with CouponChief association, and they have to start over to earn on subsequent purchases). (4).
You type a new address (URL) in the window opened by a CouponChief Stores or Offers page; (5).
You click on any "special offers" or toolbars from other websites while shopping; (6).
You allow too much time to lapse between linking to the merchant and completing the purchase. (7).
You contact the merchant to change an order. (8).
You fail to abide by the restrictions imposed by a Participating Merchant. (9).
You do not reside in the United States, Canada, or UK. (10).
The Participating Merchant fails to transmit to CouponChief the commission corresponding to the transaction. (11).
