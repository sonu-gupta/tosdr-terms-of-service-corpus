Wolt is a Finnish technology company known for its food-delivery platform.
Customers can order food from the platform's restaurant partners via mobile apps or a web browser, and either pick it up or have it delivered by the platform's courier partners.

Timeline[edit]

2014: Wolt was founded in Helsinki, Finland.
2015: Wolt first launched in Helsinki as pick-up only.
2016: Wolt added delivery to the platform and expanded to Sweden and Estonia.
Wolt did some experimenting with self-driving delivery robots in Tallinn in cooperation with Starship Technologies.[10]
[11]

2017: Wolt launched in Denmark, Latvia and Lithuania.
2018: Wolt launched in Croatia, Czech Republic, Norway, Hungary, Georgia, Israel and Poland.
2019: Wolt launched in Serbia, Greece, Azerbaijan, Slovakia and Kazakhstan.
2020: Wolt launched in Japan and Germany.
