
         Privacy Statement
When do we collect personal information?
We collect personal information when you correspond with us.
For example, when you forward the details of a scam to us, we will record your email address.
What is your personal information is used for?
We collect your personal information in order to provide you with email communications relevant to the Re:scam project, or other relevant content about scams.
