and we use "session ID cookies" to enable certain features of the Sites, to better understand how you interact with the Sites and to monitor aggregate usage and web traffic routing on the Sites.
You can instruct your browser, by changing its options, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit.
If you do not accept cookies, however, you may not be able to use all portions of the Sites or all functionality of our services.
