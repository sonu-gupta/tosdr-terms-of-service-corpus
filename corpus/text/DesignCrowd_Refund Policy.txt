 1 Our Refund Policy 
 1.1 Multiple Designer / Creative Contest refunds - Client is eligible for and can request a full refund of its Project Budget (excluding any Project Posting Fees and Upgrade Fees paid to Us and excluding the applicable transaction fee(s)) in respect of a Project if: 
 (a) it requests a refund in writing using the procedure on the DesignCrowd Site ("Refund Request").

 (b) it has not selected a Winning Work from the Works submitted (if any) in response to a Brief.

 (c) the Multiple Designer / Creative Contest is not a Guaranteed Multiple Designer Contest.
and 
 (d) the Refund Request is made within 60 days of launching the relevant Project.

 1.3 Exceptions - Notwithstanding the terms above, Client is not eligible for and will not receive any refund where: 
 (a) the Project / Contest is Guaranteed and therefore ineligible for a refund.

 (b) Client has already selected a Winning Work.
