Violence
Abuse

Illegal/unlawful content

Nudity

Manipulate other users
Create a large amount of Accounts
Share excessive amounts of content of any type ("Spam")

If your account got suspended, you are not allowed to create any new accounts. Impersonation You are not allowed to impersonate other people on SpaceHey.
We understand that some of you like to create Profiles with the names of popular persons as a parody.
If you do something like this, please use the "About me" section on your Profile to inform other users that your Account is a parody account.
You must do this in a way which can be easily seen and understood by other users.
