Services for Students with Disabilities (SSD): Students and parents generally work with their school to request accommodations and provide information to College Board.

When a paper Student Eligibility Form is used to request accommodations, the parent/guardian must indicate on the form they consent to the request/release of information.
Students 18 and over may sign the forms themselves.
Visit our Services for Students with Disabilities page to learn more.

English Learner (EL) Supports: Students requesting EL supports must demonstrate their EL status.
