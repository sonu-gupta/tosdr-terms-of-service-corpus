There is often no privacy policy and users are forced to trust that the instance operator is not collecting data.

Searches are kept private.

 phonelink Device Type (User agent) - This is what browser, device, and operating system you are using.

 settings Diagnostics - When this data is only collected when there is an error or only a short amount of time while diagnosing an issue.
