
         Privacy Policy
Dr.
Rubisco and his website do not collect, store, or share any data from their visitors, public or private, regardless of personal identity.
Any information used to run the site on the client's device is requested as needed, and is not stored, sent, shared, or collected in any way.

No client visiting the site is tracked, and each visit to the site is indistinguishable from another.
All users remain completely anonymous to the managerial staff of the website and their associates.
