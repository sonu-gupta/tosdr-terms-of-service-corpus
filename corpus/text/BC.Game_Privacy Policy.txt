 Cookies Policy 1.What are cookies?
 A cookie is a piece of information in the form of a very small text file that is placed on an internet user's computer.
It is generated by a web page server (which is basically the computer that operates the website) and can be used by that server whenever the user visits the site.
A cookie can be thought of as an internet user's identification card, which tells a website when the user has returned.
Cookies can't harm your computer and we don't store any personally identifiable information about you on any of our cookies.
