Your Rights

Right to Delete.
You have the right to request that we delete Personal Information that we have collected from you.


Right to Opt Out.
You have the right to opt-out of the sale of your Personal Information.
We will not discriminate against you in any manner prohibited by applicable law for exercising these rights.
