What are cookies?
Cookies are small text files that the websites visited by the user send to their computers.
Cookies are memorised on devices (such as computers, tablets, smartphones and on any appliance used to surf the web) and at next visit of the same user are resent to the relative websites.
To know more we suggest you to read our cookie policy, which is available here (and the privacy policy of our website, which can be read at the following link: https://www.tintolav.com/en/privacy.html ).
You can choose what cookies to accept and go on, or else you can configure the single cookies by following the below-mentioned directions.
There are various types of cookies, some to make the use of the website more effective, others to enable certain functions.
