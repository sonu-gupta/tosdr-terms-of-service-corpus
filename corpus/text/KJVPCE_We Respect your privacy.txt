 Dyslexia-Friendly Bible 
 Our King James Bible website now supports optimal fonts and colors to improve readability for people with dyslexia.
To activate this feature, please follow the instructions below:

Go to the settings menu.
Select OpenDyslexic under "Font Family".
Click the green "Update" button near the bottom of the screen.
