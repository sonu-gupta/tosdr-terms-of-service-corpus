Web Measurement, Site Operation and Customization


We use web measurement and customization technologies such as session and persistent cookies to improve website functions for visitors and to better understand how the public is using our website.

"Session Cookies" 

Some services on our website use "session cookies." These are:

Small bits of text temporarily stored in your computer.
Used to aid searching and navigating the site.
Deleted when you close your browser.

 Your device retains data stored in our session cookies only for as long as needed to use for measurement, operation and customization purposes.


Example: Temporarily storing site data such as shopping cart items for the duration of your visit.


"Persistent Cookies"

Some services on our website use multi-session cookies, also known as "persistent cookies." Persistent cookies are:

Small bits of text temporarily stored in your computer.
Placed on your computer for more than a single session.
Used to differentiate between new and returning site visitors, and to customize our website for frequent visitors.

 Your device retains data stored in our persistent cookies for up to two years after your last sign-in to our website.


Example: At your request, storing your preferences across browser sessions so you don't have to enter them each time you visit the site.


Use and Sharing of Cookie Data

 We don't use session cookies or persistent cookies to track you across the Internet or collect Personally identifiable information (PII) and we don't access session cookies created by other sites.


How to Opt-Out or Disable Cookies

 Both session and persistent cookies pertaining to our website are enabled by default because of their integration with our website.
If you do not wish to have session or persistent cookies placed on your computer, you can disable them using your Web browser.
If you opt out of cookies, you will not have access to all information and resources at STORYCHECKS.com.
Instructions for disabling or opting out of cookies in the most popular browsers are located at http://www.wikihow.com/Disable-Cookies.

 Note: If you disable cookies in your browser, it may cause problems with searching and displaying information at sites outside of STORYCHECKS.com as well.
