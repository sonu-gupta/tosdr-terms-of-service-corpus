


Online Return Policy:





How do I return items?




If you are not completely satisfied with your AR DESIGNS STL online purchase, we will gladly accept the return of unworn, unwashed items with tags still attached within 14 days of your delivery date. Once we have received the return within the 14-day window, we will gladly issue a refund back to your original form of payment.
Please note any shipping charges will not be refunded.
