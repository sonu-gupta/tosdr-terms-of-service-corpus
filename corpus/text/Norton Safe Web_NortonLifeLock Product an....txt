 Enrollment and Billing 
 Product Specific Privacy Statements 

The enrollment and billing privacy statement provides our customers with a detailed look at how personal data is collected and processed when enrolling for a NortonLifeLock service. For more information about our data processing practices and any rights you may have, please see our Global Privacy Statement.
Norton Enrollment Process
The Norton Enrollment process applies to all customers who are purchasing their Norton products and standalone features through the Norton eStore or by calling into a Norton contact center.
This information is only collected once and is used to set up the Norton Account.
Additional information may be collected at the discretion of the customer in order to maintain the account during the subscription to the product. &nbsp.
Categories of Data and Personal Data Collected
Purpose of Processing Your Personal Data
Data You Provide:
User Data may include:
Email
Norton account password
Name (if given)
Country (if given)
Phone number (if given)
National ID (region specific, if outside the United States)
Physical address
Tax ID (region specific, if outside the United States)

User Data: This data is processed to authenticate, administer, and maintain the account and product subscription as well as to communicate with you. Data We Collect/Access:
User Data may include:
IP address

Device ID

Administrative data including:
Norton account ID

User Data: This data is processed for the purposes of delivering the content in accordance with your device(s), determining appropriate language settings for communicating with you, and monitoring website activity from you (cookies).

Administrative Data: This data is used and generated internally at NortonLifeLock for the purpose of tracking account activity between internal company systems, applications, and architecture.
Data We Collect/Access from Third Parties:
NortonLifeLock does not collect any other data for enrollment processes from any third parties.
NortonLifeLock does not collect any other data for enrollment processes from any third parties.
Norton Billings Process
The Norton Billing process applies to all customers who have purchased their Norton products and/or standalone features through the Norton eStore or by calling into a Norton contact center.
This information is used to bill the customers for their services and products on a regular interval.
For products purchased through other methods, such as Norton 360 App for Apple and Android, this information will not apply. &nbsp.

User Data: This data is processed to bill the account for products and features purchased.

Security Data: This data is processed by third parties and NortonLifeLock to validate that an authenticated payment transaction occurred, and the payment card and account are appropriate for billing.
