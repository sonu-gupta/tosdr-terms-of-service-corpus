Closed Captioning Policy Fox News is pleased to offer closed captioning on many Fox News programs made available online and through mobile apps.
Programs with closed captioning will have a "CC" icon in the player controls.
Just tap to enable or disable.
Certification Statement
To report an issue or concern regarding closed captioning on foxnews.com, please contact us at closedcaptioning@foxnews.com.
Please detail your issue or concern by providing the following information:

Name of the program
Type of issue (e.g., garbling, missing captions on a certain episode, captions cut off at certain times or on certain days)
Website or application where you viewed the program
Approximate time and date that the issue occurred
Your name and contact information including mailing address, email and phone number
