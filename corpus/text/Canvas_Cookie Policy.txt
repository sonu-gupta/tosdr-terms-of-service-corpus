 What is a cookie?Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device.
They are widely used in order to make websites work, or work in a better, more efficient way.
They can do this because websites can read and write these files, enabling them to recognize you and remember important information that will make your use of a website more convenient (e.g.
by remembering your user preferences).2.
