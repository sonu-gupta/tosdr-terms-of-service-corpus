
        Return PolicyReturn Policy We accept returns on new and unused items purchased directly from this website.
The request must be made within 14 days of delivery.

To qualify for a return, proof of purchase is required.
Buyers are responsible for return shipping costs.
If the item is not returned in its original condition, the buyer is responsible for any loss in value.
Once your return is received, we will send an email to notify you of the status of your refund.
If you are approved, your refund will be processed to your original method of payment.
Refunds do not include the original shipping charge.
