What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files sent from a website and stored in your browser or device.
They contain a string of text that contains information about your browser.
Some cookies can contain personally identifiable information such as name, address, IP address, etc.

What kind of cookies are in use?


Session Cookies are cookies that are erased when the user closes the Web browser.
The session cookie is stored in temporary memory and is not retained after the browser is closed.
Session cookies do not collect information from the userâs computer.

Persistent Cookies are stored on your device between site visits.
They allow us to remember your preferences.

First-Party Cookies are cookies created by our site for use while on our site.
Third-Party Cookies are cookies created by third-parties outside of our site.
