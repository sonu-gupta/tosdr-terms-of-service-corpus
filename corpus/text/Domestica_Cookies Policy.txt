Cookies
A cookie is a file that is downloaded to your terminal/device (PC or mobile) with the purpose of storing data that may be updated and recovered by the entity that installs them.
Our Platform uses "cookies" to collect information about its operation and its users' experience.
We do not store sensible personally identifiable information such as your address, passwords, or credit/debit card information in the cookies we use.
Purpose
Cookies can track information about your visits to our Platform, their frequency and length, the pages viewed and the time spent in our Platform.
We may use cookies to:


Identify problems to improve our Platform


Enable you to better navigate our Platform


Offer you a better experience in our Platform


Set your apart from other users


We also ask for your consent to use cookies to allow us to obtain more information about your preferences and customize our Platform to meet your interests.

Our own cookies
