 What are cookies? 
 A cookie is a small text file that gets saved on your device when you visit our website.
It allows the website to remember your actions and preferences (such as browser language, font size, and other display preferences) over time.
This information improves website performance and helps us to optimise our website on an ongoing basis.

 Modern web browsers support cookies, and a majority of websites use them too.
Note that you can always manage your browser settings to block certain cookies or indicate when a cookie is being used.
However, when adjusting cookie settings, remember that some website elements and features may malfunction without the use of cookies.
