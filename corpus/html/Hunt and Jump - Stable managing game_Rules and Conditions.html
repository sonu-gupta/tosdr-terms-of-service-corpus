★Achievement Tasks Create a 3 year old stallion + Show Instructions

Use the "Create A Horse" page to create a stallion that is 3 years of age.

  1. Under "Stable Home" in the navigation bar click on the "Create A Horse" link.
  2. Fill out the form in the "Create a New Horse" section.
    • Select a unique name. You can add your initials or stable name, if the name you want is already taken.
    • The # sign can also be used to automatically add the ID number to the horse name.
    • Select "Colt" as your gender. A colt is a young stallion.
    • Select any height or size you wish.
    • Make sure "3yr Old" is selected in the age drop down.
  3. Hit "Create New Foal"
  4. Once created you can click the image to see your new horse's page, or navigate to "My Barns" to see a list of all horses you own.
  5. Return here to claim your prize!
0 of 1 Reward:
12.5% towards next bonus stall.

Put a horse through "Breeding Advice" + Show Instructions

Run a horse through the "Breeding Advice" test.

  1. Select an untested mare or stallion you own. Select the "Control Panel" tab.
  2. Click the button that reads "Breeding Advice"
  3. The results of the test will pop up and tell you if your horse is worth breeding too, if not your horse will be automatically altered.
  4. Most horses will not pass the test.
  5. You need to own lots of altered show horses to make money, so that is a good thing!
  6. Return here to claim your prize!
