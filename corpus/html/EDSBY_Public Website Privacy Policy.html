This Policy applies to visitors of our public website (www.edsby.com) (“Website”). . Edsby’s public Website, operated by CoreFour Inc. (“we”, “us”, or “our”), may collect Website visitor’s Personal Information, including those of clients or prospective clients, all referred to in this Public Website Privacy Policy as “you” or “your”.

Other Links:

Our public Website contains links to other websites and services that we do not own or operate. By clicking on those links, you will leave our Website. Those third-party websites and services are governed by separate and independent privacy policies, which we recommend you read carefully.

“Personal Information” is any information provided to us or generated within the public Website that personally identifies an individual, such as name or email address, or other information which could be reasonably linked to such identifying information. Personal Information does not include de-identified information (i.e., information that does not identify you) such as usage data.

You may voluntarily provide us with Personal Information, such as your name, email address, phone number and company name, when you subscribe to our mailing list, fill in a sales lead form, make an inquiry or respond to an offer on our public Website.

Like most websites, when you visit our public Website, we may receive information about your visit and interaction with our Website. 9.0 Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our public Website, like most other websites, uses cookies and similar technologies. “Cookies” are small text files installed on your computer hard drive or web browser when you visit or use our Website. Most browsers accept cookies by default.

What information do we collect using cookies?

Most cookies collect information that is not identifiable to you. They help us collect information so we can better understand how you access our Website and therefore improve the design, layout, and content of our Website. They also remember visitor preferences, such as language preferences, so that the user can have a better experience.

Some cookies on our public Website are set by us (i.e., first-party cookies) and some cookies are set by third parties (i.e., third-party cookies), such as Bablic for managing translated versions of our Website, FlowXO for automated user support chats on our public website, and LeadFWD for tracking whether you have filled in one of our Website forms, and if so, what subsequent interactions you have had with our marketing content.

We also use Google Analytics to measure traffic or Remarketing with Analytics to deliver Edsby-related ads to you on websites in Google’s Remarketing network. To learn more about how Google Analytics collects and uses data, as well as how you can control information sent to Google, please visit this Google site.

To learn more about cookies, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

How can you manage cookies?

Although most browsers accept cookies, you can set your browser to reject cookies. You will need to follow the instructions contained in your browser’s help file (usually located within the “Help,” “Tools,” or “Edit” settings). If you have more than one browser, the opt-out or settings you set will only apply to that specific browser and not the others. If you choose to disable cookies, some of the features and services on our Website might not function properly.
