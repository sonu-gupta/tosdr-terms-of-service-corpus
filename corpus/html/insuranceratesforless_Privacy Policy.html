Information We Collect and How We Use It

We will collect and use personal information about you that you enter on our Website to respond to your request for products and services offered through this Site. We do not collect any personally identifiable information from you unless you voluntarily choose to disclose such information while taking advantage of the services available on this Site. When completing the form on our Website, you may be asked to enter personal information such as your first and last name, street address, email address, phone number, etc. We also may collect general information such as your gender, age, birth date, zipcode, etc. Any data we request that is not required will be specified as voluntary or optional.

When you use the Website for any service we offer, we may also collect non-personally identifiable data about your activity when visiting the Website, including the pages you view, your preference settings, the IP address assigned to the computer you use when visiting the Website, the type of browser you use, your operating system, and whether you reached our website directly or by clicking on a link. This data is not personally identifiable. It helps us determine the extent to which our visitors use certain parts of the Website, which, in turn, enables us to make the Website as useful as possible.

We also track the number of visitors to our Website that come from one of our promotional partners’ websites and the identity of the promotional partner that directed the individual to our Website. We record this information to determine if our online marketing or promotional campaigns are successful.

Additionally, we record your Internet Protocol (IP) addresses when you visit our Website. An IP Address is a number that is used by other computers to identify your computer when you log on to the Internet.

To assist us in providing services on the Website, we use third party vendors, who use various technologies, including scripts and cookies, to conduct tracking, analytics, and related services. One of these providers is Lead Intelligence, Inc. d/b/a Jornaya, whose privacy policy you can view here: https://www.jornaya.com/consumer-privacy-policy.

We use these vendors’ technologies and the information collected to administer the Website, analyze trends, measure ad performance, track users’ movements around the site (including the capture of visual snapshots of your activity on our site), and gather demographic information about our user base. We may receive reports based on the use of these technologies by these companies on an individual as well as aggregated basis.

Do We Use Cookies?

Yes. Cookies are small files that can be transferred to your computer’s hard drive through your Web browser. Cookies enable us to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information that you previously shared with us, such as your setting preferences. We use cookies to understand and save your preferences for future visits, to keep track of advertisements, to track visitors through our Website, and to compile data about site traffic and site interaction so that we can offer a better experience in the future. If you prefer, you can choose to have your browser notify you each time a cookie is being sent or to refuse cookies altogether. If you turn your cookies off, some of our services may not function properly.

We may use your personally identifiable information to provide you our services, including when you request to be connected with insurance providers. We do this by sharing your information with third parties so they may correspond with you and provide you with information that you have requested. Please be advised that insuranceratesforless.com is not an insurance company or provider.

We may use your non-personal information to personalize and improve your experience on our Website. to respond to your communications with us. to improve our customer service. for internal research, recordkeeping, and reporting purposes. and for our own marketing purposes.
