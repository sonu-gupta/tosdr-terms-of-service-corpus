OSable Privacy Policy

At OSable, the privacy of our users is of paramount concern in a world where personal data is being sold to advertisers on a regular basis and targeted advertising is common-place.

We do not operate with this business model.

We instead operate on the model that relies on donations from our satisfied users of our tool

(N.B. We do not currently accept 'donations' but this is being developed at the moment).

We are a community project, so we feel this is the most ethical method we could utilise. Consider this our promise.

 

Privacy when interacting with OSable third-parties

It is to be noted that we do not collect your personal information from your device *, however companies such as Google-owned YouTube or Reddit will if you visit them from our site which hosts some of our content or click on some videos embedded on our site from our YouTube channel.

* Excluding the data that is input into our tool's forms by users and basic analytics data for understanding the volume of users visiting the site.

 

More Information

If you would like more information on a topic regarding to user privacy, please contact us via our contact page and we would be delighted to assist you with your query.

 

This policy may be subject to change at OSable's discretion at anytime.
