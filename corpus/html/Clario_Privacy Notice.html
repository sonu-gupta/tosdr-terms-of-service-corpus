2. And, what does this Privacy Notice apply to?

This Privacy Notice applies whenever you visit our website at https://clario.co (“Website”).

3. What kinds of data do we process?

Well, this is the data we receive from you. While visiting our website, we may collect your basic contact details such as name, telephone number and email.

We collect these categories of personal data because we have a legitimate business interest in providing you with our services. We use this personal data to communicate with you, provide support and assistance, respond to your inquiries, resolve disputes, and collect feedback.

We may contact you via email to tell you about promotions, products or services if you separately have agreed to this, changes to this Notice, or to respond to an inquiry you have made. You may opt out of future contact from us at any time by using the method provided in the email (such as clicking on a link), or contacting us via the email address provided in this Notice.

Data that we receive automatically. When you enter our website, in addition to the data you enter yourself, we may collect some information automatically, i.e. cookies, in order to optimize your experience when using our website.

A cookie is a small text file that helps a website remember your actions and preferences. We use some cookies to operate our website. You can turn off cookies, but then you won’t have access to some essential features of our website.

Please read our Cookie Policy carefully for more details about the information we collect when you use our website.

Children’s data. Our website is not aimed at children under the age of 16, and we may not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone under the age of 16. If you believe that we have collected personal data from minors, please contact us via email hello@weareclario.com.

4. Why do we process your data?

We use data for the fast and efficient rendering of services to our customers. Also, we use your data for other reasons such as safety and security, customer support, communications from the Company, compliance with legal obligations, also for research and development.

5. Do we share or disclose your data?

We may share your data with some companies, consultants and contractors which we hire to provide limited services on our behalf and which value data privacy as much as we do. We will only provide those companies with personal data they need to deliver the service. Also, we may disclose your data if we receive a governmental request and when it is required by applicable law. Or we may share data with your consent. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to anyone.

We will not sell your personal data to any company or organization, but we may transfer your personal data to a successor entity upon a merger, consolidation or other corporate reorganization in which we may participate or to a purchaser or acquirer of all or a substantial part of Company’s assets to which this application relates. In such an event, we will notify you before your personal data is transferred and becomes subject to a different Privacy Notice.

6. Do we transfer your data?

We may need to process your data outside of the country in which you reside. We apply measures to protect our data transfers and use all appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses adopted by the European Commission.

7. How do we handle your data?

The security of your personal data is very important to us, and we do our best to secure it.

Data security. We protect your data by using physical, technical, and administrative security measures to reduce the risks of loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, and alteration. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy policies of other websites and services that may be linked from our website.

Data storage. We will retain your personal data for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Notice unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

8. How can you manage your data?

If you would like to access, review, update, rectify, and delete any personal data we hold about you, simply contact us using the details below, to review and manage privacy settings:

  • email: hello@weareclario.com.

Also, you can exercise a right to object or restrict processing and data portability. Please, address inquiries for the execution of these rights to hello@weareclario.com. In case you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you have a right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Access. You can request an explanation of the personal data we process about you. Also, you can request a copy of your personal data undergoing processing.

Rectification. You can correct any inaccurate data about you, or you can update your personal data.

Data portability. You have a right to receive the personal data concerning you, which you provided to us. You can make a request to transmit this data directly to another data controller in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format. We will transmit your data directly to another controller in cases where it is technically feasible.

Erasure. You have a right to delete all personal data that you have provided to us. Note, we may retain certain information as required by law and for legitimate business purposes permitted by law.

Restrict processing. You can request us to temporarily or permanently stop processing all or some of your personal data.

Object processing. You can, at any time, object to the processing of your personal data on grounds relating to your particular situation. For example, you have a right to object to your personal data being processed for direct marketing purposes.

We make these disclosures to those visiting our website who reside in California which supersede and replace any conflicting disclosures found elsewhere on our website.

Opt-out of disclosure for direct marketing purposes. You may request such information by contacting us by email addressed to hello@weareclario.com. Please reference California Privacy Rights in your subject line. You may also write to us at Unit No: 3005-D6, Swiss Tower, Plot No: JLT-PH2-Y3A, Jumeirah Lakes Towers, Dubai, UAE.

Automatic gathering of information. We collect data that you provide to us online, and through websites of unaffiliated third parties.

Automatic gathering of information by third parties. When you visit our website, third parties can collect personal data about your online activities over time and across different websites pertaining to your visit to or use of our and other websites.

Do-not-track requests. California residents visiting our website may request that we do not automatically gather and track information pertaining to their online browsing movements across the Internet. We currently do not have the ability to honor these requests. We may modify this Notice as our abilities change.
