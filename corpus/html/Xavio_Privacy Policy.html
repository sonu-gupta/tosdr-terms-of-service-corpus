Privacy Policy Overview

Xavio Infotech (P) Ltd. is firmly committed to the privacy of its members. Xavio Infotech (P) Ltd. works hard to earn and keep your trust, so it adheres to the following principles to protect your privacy:

  • Xavio Infotech (P) Ltd. does not rent or sell your personally identifiable information to third parties.

  • Any personally identifiable information that you provide to Xavio Infotech (P) Ltd. will be secured with industry standard protocols and technology.
Information Collection

Xavio Infotech (P) Ltd. is an online marketplace that connects businesses with freelancers. When you visit Xavio Infotech (P) Ltd. you provide two types of information: personal information you knowingly choose to disclose that is collected by Xavio Infotech (P) Ltd. and Web Site use information collected by Xavio Infotech (P) Ltd. as you interact with the Xavio Infotech (P) Ltd. web site.

When you register with Xavio Infotech (P) Ltd. as a user (Freelancer or as an Employer), you provide certain personal information, including, your name, your email address and your address. Additionally, when you establish your profile as a user (Freelancer or as an Employer) you will provide information relating to your skills, experience, certificates, skills ,awards . Such information does not identify the specificity of the individual member. Freelancers are not required to, but have the option to display their contact information (i.e. name, address, phone number, email address, etc.) to Employers who conduct searches of the Xavio Infotech (P) Ltd. database.

When you enter the Website, Xavio Infotech (P) Ltd. collects your IP address. This information is gathered for all Xavio Infotech (P) Ltd. visitors. In addition, Xavio Infotech (P) Ltd. stores certain information from your browser using "cookies." A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's computer tied to information about the user. Xavio Infotech (P) Ltd. uses session ID cookies to confirm that users are logged in. These cookies terminate once the user closes the browser. By default, Xavio Infotech (P) Ltd. uses a persistent cookie that stores your login ID (but not your password) to make it easier for you to login when you return to the Website. Xavio Infotech (P) Ltd. members must have cookies enabled on their browser. Note, Cookie preferences are set within each browser's Internet options/preferences.

Xavio Infotech (P) Ltd. is the sole owner of the information collected through the Website.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for a variety of reasons detailed below. Disabling Cookies

You can prevent the setting of cookies by adjusting the settings on your browser (see your browser Help for how to do this). Be aware that disabling cookies will affect the functionality of this and many other websites that you visit. Disabling cookies will usually result in also disabling certain functionality and features of the this site. Therefore it is recommended that you do not disable cookies.
