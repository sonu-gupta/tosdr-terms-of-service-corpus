Member Account Set Up and Earning Points

To participate,and to fully set up your PRPP account and become a PRPP member (“Member”), customer must fully complete required account information at pointsrewardsplus.com and must first have a valid Kroger Plus Card account. If a customer does not have a Plus Card account, click here to create one: https://www.kroger.com/account/create/.

PRPP is a customer loyalty program that allows participants to earn one thousand (1,000) points (“Points”) for every $30 spent on purchases of qualifying products (full list of qualifying products available at pointsrewardsplus.com/products) (“Qualifying Products”) (while supplies last) during each Qualifying Period (defined below) at participating Kroger stores, thru in-store and/or ecommerce transactions, using their individual Plus Card account. Each time, during each Qualifying Period, a customer can earn one thousand (1,000) Points based on purchases of Qualifying Products when using their individual Plus Card account, and every time they spend $30 in purchases of Qualifying Products, the customer will subsequently receivea unique one-time use PRPP pin code (“Pin Code”), which is redeemable in their account for one thousand (1,000) Points. Customer must spend the full $30.00 on Qualifying Products prior to the conclusion of a Qualifying Period to earn their one thousand (1,000) Points. Amounts spent less than $30.00 prior to the conclusion of any Qualifying Period will not carry over to subsequent Qualifying Periods. Customer will receive the earned Pin Code either in-store or email as part of current transaction, next transaction or within forty-eight (48) hours, except for Qualifying Period #1 (see * below for details). Each customer that receives a Pin Code must accurately deposit that Pin Code in his/her individualized, fully operating PRPP account which they have created at pointsrewardsplus.com to secure their Points. Individuals may earn multiple Pin Codes in any one transaction or over the course of any Qualifying Period but are limited to depositing four (4) Pin Codes per week and redeeming two (2) Rewards per week.

The specific Qualifying Products participating in the PRPP may change frequently during the program. For the current list of Qualifying Products participating in the PRPP at any time, go to pointsrewardsplus.com/products. 

There are eight (8) separate and independent qualifying periods (“Qualifying Periods” or “QP”) to purchase Qualifying Products and earn potential Points as follows:

- QP #1: Beginning at 12:00:00 AM ET on February 16, 2022 and ending on at 11:59:59 PM ET on March 31, 2022*;
- QP #2: Beginning at 12:00:00 AM ET on April 1, 2022 and ending at 11:59:59 PM ET on April 30, 2022;
- QP #3: Beginning at 12:00:00 AM ET on May 1, 2022 and ending at 11:59:59 PM ET on May 31, 2022;
- QP #4: Beginning at 12:00:00 AM ET on June 1, 2022 and ending at 11:59:59 PM ET on June 30, 2022;
- QP #5: Beginning at 12:00:00 AM ET on August 17, 2022 and ending at 11:59:59 PM ET on September 30, 2022;
- QP #6: Beginning at 12:00:00 AM ET on October 1, 2022 and ending at 11:59:59 PM ET on October 31, 2022;
- QP #7: Beginning at 12:00:00 AM ET on November 1, 2022 and ending at 11:59:59 PM ET on November 30, 2022. and
- QP #8: Beginning at 12:00:00 AM ET on December 1, 2022 and ending at 11:59:59 PM ET on January 3, 2023.

*For all participating that make qualifying purchase(s) to earn one thousand (1,000) Points by spending $30.00 on Qualifying Products using their individual Plus Card account between 12:00:00 AM ET on February 16, 2022 thru 11:59:59 PM ET on March 1, 2022 (“Initial Period”), either in-store or online will not receive their Pin Code(s) unless they make a purchase at a participating store (see list below) after 12:00:00 AM ET on March 2, 2022. Thus, if a participant earns a Pin Code during the Initial Period, they will not receive their Pin Code until they return to a participating Kroger store and make a purchase in which case they will receive any Pin Code(s) earned during the Initial Period on their first shopping trip after the conclusion of the Initial Period. The Pin Code(s) earned will appear on the participant’s printout during check-out. If a participant would like to receive their earned Pin Code(s) for a qualifying purchases made during the Initial Period but does not want to have to return to the store and make an additional shopping trip, they may contact our service provider to receive their earned Pin Code(s) using the following contact information and submitting receipt showing qualifying purchase(s): PRPP Customer Service, 10150 Mallard Creek Road, Suite 500, Charlotte, NC 28262. or Customerservice@pointsrewardsplus.com.

Members can redeem Points from their individual PRPP account anytime from beginning at/about 12:00:00 AM ET on March 2, 2022 to 11:59:59 PM ET on February 3, 2023 (“Redemption Period”). The maximum number of Rewards a Member can redeem per week within the Redemption Period is two (2) Rewards per week.

To validate Pin Codes received, claim potential Points and be eligible to redeem for Rewards, Member must access their individualized PRPP account by going to pointsrewardsplus.com and correctly follow all steps including submitting each Pin Code received to claim and deposit Points in their account. Each eligible Pin Code issued will expire thirty (30) days from date of print. Kroger has the sole right to make the final decision on whether a purchase qualifies for Points under the PRPP.

Processing of a Pin Code and posting Points to Members’ account takes approximately forty-eight (48) hours from the time the Pin Code information was correctly submitted into Member’s account and received by Kroger. Once a Member’s Points are successfully posted, Member will receive a notification confirming whether submission has been successfully validated or a reason why such submission could not be processed at such time, as determined by Kroger, in its sole and absolute discretion. Members are responsible for ensuring that their Points are properly credited. If proper credit does not appear on the Member's activity statement, the Member should contact PRPP Customer Service within thirty (30) days of the purchase (Customerservice@pointsrewardsplus.com. 833-638-3490). Points subsequently determined, in the sole discretion of Kroger, to be invalid, will be removed from a Member’s Point accumulation total.

Once Pin Code(s) are deposited in Member’s account and Points are validated, Points can be redeemed for eligible and available Rewards (defined below) (up to the maximum of two (2) Rewards per week), until 11:59:59 PM ET on February 3, 2023 at, or as long as Rewards supply last or termination of the PRPP.
