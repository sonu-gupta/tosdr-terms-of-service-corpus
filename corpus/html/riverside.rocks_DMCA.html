If a service or a user of a service has infringed on your copyright, you can submit a DMCA. The following must be included in the DMCA you send:

  • Your name, street address, telephone number, and email address
  • Details about copyrighted work that you claim has been infringed
  • A statement acknowledging you are acting in good faith
  • Optionally submit server logs

This information can be sent to support /at/ riverside[.]rocks with the subject "DMCA".

False DMCAs are a crime! Submit only accurate information, please.

See also: Reporting Abuse
