This section pertains to the Privacy Policy of wingify.com (hereinafter referred to as "Website" or "Wingify"). We value the trust you place in wingify.com. For this reason, we endeavor and insist upon the highest standards for security of personal information.

The protection and security of your personal information is among our top priorities. This Privacy Policy discloses our practice with respect to the information collected from the users of this Website for commercial purposes. By using this Website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy (“Usage Terms”) of the Website. By accessing or using this Website, you expressly consent to our use and disclosure of your personal information in any manner described in this Privacy Policy.

The Privacy Policy covers the following areas. This list is not all-inclusive and is subject to further updates in the future:

1. Collection of Personally Identifiable Information

This Privacy Policy extends to both, the users who visit the Website but do not transact business on the Website and the users who are Registered on the Website. “Personal Information” refers to any information that identifies or can be used to identify, contact, or locate the person, to whom such information pertains including, but not limited to, name, phone number, and email address.

2. Use of Cookies

A "cookie" is a small piece of information stored by a Web server on a Web browser so that it can be later read from that browser. Cookies are useful for enabling the browser to remember information specific to a given user. The website wingify.com places both permanent and temporary cookies in your computer hard disk drive. Website cookies do not contain any of your personally identifiable information.

3. Use of Demographic and Profile Data

In our efforts to continually improve our product and service offerings, we collect and analyze demographic and profile data about user activity on our website. We identify and use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our website.

4. Security Precautions

Our site has stringent security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. Whenever you change or access your account information, we offer the use of a secure server. After your information is in our possession, we adhere to strict security guidelines, protecting these against unauthorized access.

5. Questions

Questions regarding this statement should be directed to privacy@wingify.com.

These disclosures are made in good faith and belief that such disclosure is reasonably necessary for enforcing these Usage Terms. for complying with the applicable laws and regulations.

We also ensure that these recipients of such information agree to process such information based on our instructions and in compliance with this Privacy Policy and any other appropriate confidentiality and security measures.
