1. What we collect

 

We gather information about you in a range of ways.

Personal Information. We may from time to time ask you for Personal Information, and we may collect certain information from your device each time you visit our site. We may also receive Personal Information from third parties with whom we have a business relationship. Such Personal Information may be added to existing information we gather on this site and or the app and such Personal Information will not violate any existing privacy or terms between you and the third parties or this policy as amended.

"Personal Information" is defined as personally identifiable information and includes, but is not limited to, name. address. e-mail address. SMS or text message address. phone number. credit card and other payment information. information regarding your academic and extracurricular activities and interests that may identify you. and other information that may identify you as an individual or allow online contact with you.

 

Information Automatically Collected. We automatically log information about you and your computer because it can help us identify potential technical issues as they arise. For example, when visiting our app, we log your computer operating system type, browser type, browser language, the web app you viewed before browsing to our app, pages you viewed, how long you spent on a page, access times, the number of actions performed on our app, the type of actions performed on our app, the locale you are visiting our app from and information about your use of and actions on our app. Such information is “de-identified” and is not linked to any other personal information nor is it used for third-party commercial purposes beyond the services we offer on this site and app and the improvement of current and future products.

 

Cookies. We use tracking technology known as “cookies” on this site and on the app including application IDs and a user ID to monitor use and traffic routing as well as to customize and improve our products.

 

Session Cookies. We use “session cookies”, which improve user experience by storing certain information from your current visit on your device, such as log-in information. These enable us to remember your login session so you can move easily within this site or other services.

 

Performance Cookies. Partners and analytics companies may also put cookies on your device. We may share with partners a cryptographic hash of a common account identifier (such as a User ID), to help us measure services provided to you. Please note that this Privacy Policy covers only our use of cookies and does not include the use of cookies by any third parties.

 

Location based service. In order to provide the services, Everydae requires access to the location data of your device as determined by your IP address. Such location data is of express business necessity to Everydae and will not display personal location but rather the location of the device on which services were accessed. You can turn your device's location data on and off at any time through your device settings. 

 

Address book and friends. Everydae is the most fun when you're interacting with your friends! Because of this, we may ask for permission to access your address book. This will give you the ability to add friends that are already on Everydae as well as invite pals in your address book who have not yet joined Everydae. If you're inviting friends to Everydae keep in mind that you'll probably be inviting them via text message, so be aware that your carrier may charge you for sending those text messages depending on your phone plan. Also, when inviting friends, please be considerate and don't spam your entire address book.
