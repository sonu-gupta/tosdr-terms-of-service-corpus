If further verification is needed, the customer will be contacted via the email address and/or phone number provided.

The full amount of your order may be unavailable either until the billing transaction is settled, or the temporary hold expires. In the case of debit cards, temporary holds expire from 1-5 days after the transaction date depending on your bank's policy. in the case of credit cards, temporary holds may last as long as 30 days, depending on your issuing bank.

About the eRecyle Fee - VIZIO is the nations’ largest, U.S. based, manufacturer of High Definition television products. As VIZIO continues to offer the most advanced and latest technology to consumers, VIZIO is sensitive to the environmental concerns relating to disposal of old television technology and protection of the environment. As a result, VIZIO supports recycling program efforts adopted by many states, and encourages our customers to participate in “green” environmental practices. To learn more about VIZIO’s environmental efforts, please visit our website.

For California customers

In 2003, the California Electronic Waste Recycling Act was adopted to help launch environmental “e-waste” programs in the State of California. More information regarding this legislation can be found here:https://www.calrecycle.ca.gov/Electronics/RegInfo/

At the time of a VIZIO television purchase, you will be charged an e-waste recycling fee as mandated by the State of California to help fund recycling efforts. These fees are collected at the time of purchase and remitted to the state in accordance with regulatory requirements.

Effective January 1, 2017, the covered electronic waste recycling fee paid by a consumer upon the purchase of a new or refurbished covered electronic device shall be the following amounts:

  1. Five dollars ($5) for each covered electronic device with a screen size of less than 15 inches measured diagonally.
  2. Six dollars ($6) for each covered electronic device with a screen size greater than or equal to 15 inches but less than 35 inches measured diagonally.
  3. Seven dollars ($7) for each covered electronic device with a screen size greater than or equal to 35 inches measured diagonally.

For more information pertaining to recycling and e-waste fees in California, please visit https://www.calrecycle.ca.gov or call CalRecycle at 1-916-341-6300.
