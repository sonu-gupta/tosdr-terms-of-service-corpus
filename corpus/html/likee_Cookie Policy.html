What is a Cookie?

Cookies are small piece of data stored on your computer or mobile device while you are browsing. They are used to remember stateful information or to record your browsing activity, or other arbitrary pieces of information that previously enter into form fields to help us better understand your preferences and will further help you visit our website and enjoy Likee Services in a more convenient way.

We do not require that you accept cookies and you may withdraw your consent to our use of cookies at any time by adjusting your browser’s privacy settings. However, if you decline to accept cookies, some functionality on our website may be disabled and you may be unable to access certain parts of Likee Services. Unless you have adjusted your browser setting so that it will refuse cookies, our system will issue cookies when you direct your browser to our website.

What Cookies do we use?

We list out the major cookie we use on Likee Services below.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are essential to the operation of Likee Services, which cannot be turned off without substantially affecting your use of Likee Services. In another word, you may not be able to assess part or whole of Likee Services if these cookies are eliminated. These include, for example, cookies that enable a user to log in to our website and to check if a user is allowed access to a particular service or content.

Performance Cookies

These cookies are of analytical nature and often collect information about your use of Likee Services in order for us to improve the way it works. These cookies allow us to recognize and count the number of users and to see how users use and explore our website and help us to improve Likee Services. For instance, performance cookies allow us to record the difficulties you have with Likee Services, and show us whether our advertising is effective.
