HOW WE COLLECT DATA

We use different technologies to collect, process and store your information. These technologies include:

  • Log Files. A log file is a file that records events that occur in connection with your use of the Services, such as your service use data.

  • Cookies. A cookie is an industry-standard small data file stored on your device that acts as a unique tag to identify your browser. We use two types of cookies: session cookies and persistent cookies. Session cookies make it easier for you to navigate the Services and expire when you close your browser. Persistent cookies help with personalizing your experience, remembering your preferences, and supporting security features. Persistent cookies may remain on your device for extended periods of time, and generally may be controlled through your browser settings.

  • Third Party. We may embed third party scripts or put embeds in the page that send requests to external sites which may allow third parties to drop and read cookies on your browser. We cannot read or alter the cookies dropped on your browser by external sites. Your browser may allow you to disable cookies from third party sites.

  • Others. Other technology used include pixel tags, web beacons, local storage, algorithms and other similar tools on our Services or in our email messages.
