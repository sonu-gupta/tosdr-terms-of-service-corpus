Privacy Matters

Your privacy is important to us. It is the policy of Celestial Cinema LLC to respect your privacy regarding any information we collect across celestialcinema.io and other sites we own and operate.

We do not share any personally identifying information publicly or with third parties, except when required by law.

Our website links to external sites and social media platforms that we do not operate. Please be aware that we have no control over the content and practices of these sites and cannot accept responsibility or liability for their respective privacy policies.

Google Analytics

Like millions of other websites, Google Analytics is used on celestialcinema.io. Google Analytics is a piece of software that records data about our visitors. Here are some examples of data we collect:

  • Which website you came from to get here.
  • How long you stay.
  • What kind of computer or device you are using.
  • Which web browser you are using.
  • Your general geographic location.
  • And several other technical data points – none of which are personally identifiable.

This information allows us to better understand the kind of people visiting this site and what they are interested in. That is important so we can make better design and content decisions in the future.

Occasionally, we will compile aggregate statistics about the number of visitors celestialcinema.io receives and browsers being used. No personally identifying data is included in this type of reporting.

All our data collection activity falls within the bounds of the Google Analytics Terms of Service.

Opt-Out Options
