Data Collection

P3P policies declare the data they collect in groups (also referred to as “statements”). This policy contains 4 data groups. The data practices of each group will be explained separately.

Group “Access log information”

We collect the following information:

Click-stream data
HTTP protocol elements
User’s interaction with a page or resource
This data will be used for the following purposes:

Completion and support of the current activity.
Website and system administration.
Research and development.
This data will be used by ourselves and our agents‡.

The data in this group has been marked as non-identifiable. This means that there is no reasonable way for the site to identify the individual person this data was collected from.

The following explanation is provided for why this data is collected:

In order to protect ourselves and our customers against computer criminals, our web servers log non-identifying information about your visit to our site. For example, the pages you viewed are recorded, but this information is recorded in a way that leaves no reasonable means to connect it to your identity. To ensure maximum security, we retain these logs indefinitely. Other information within this group is collected for the purpose of completing the current request only. We do not retain it.

Group “Cookies”

We collect the following information:

HTTP cookies
This data will be used for the following purposes:

Completion and support of the current activity.
Website and system administration.
Anonymous user analysis.
Anonymous user profiling and decision-making.
This data will be used by ourselves and our agents‡.

The following explanation is provided for why this data is collected:

“Cookies” are small pieces of data sent by our server to your computer which stores them if your own security configuration permits it. Your computer will then retransmit the cookie(s) to our server as you browse our site.

“Session cookies” are cookies that exist during the current browser session only. We use session cookies to allow our server to maintain information about the contents of your shopping cart and to facilitate your shopping process. By logging in you also link the session cookies to your past transactions and account record, if any. We access this information during the current session to facilitate your shopping experience and to deliver products.

If you were directed to our site by an advertisement or an affiliate partner, we may use persistent cookies to track sales originating from these sources. It is our practice to allow these cookies to persist for a maximum of 30 days after your last visit to our site. We do not link these cookies to your personal information.

We contract with Yahoo! Inc, Google Inc. and similar third-parties to compile anonymous statistics on how our website is used by visitors. These third-parties issue cookies from our pages in order to collect this data. Personal data is neither collected by these third-parties nor provided to them by us.

Group “Transaction info (required)”

We collect the following information:

Physical contact information
Online contact information
Purchase information
Transaction history
This data will be used for the following purposes:

Completion and support of the current activity.
Website and system administration.
Research and development.
Contacting visitors for marketing of services or products. The user must opt-in to this usage.
This data will be used by ourselves and our agents‡.

The following explanation is provided for why this data is collected:

CREDIT CARD DATA: If you choose to pay by credit card, we use your credit card information only to obtain payment for the current purchase. After we submit your information for processing by your financial institution, we destroy it for your protection and for ours. Recurring payments are made possible by a mechanism which does not require us to store your credit card data.
OTHER REQUIRED DATA: To protect against credit card fraud, we require the billing address associated with your payment method. If you purchase a product which must be physically delivered, we also require your shipping address. We require your email address and telephone number to communicate with you about your purchase. To facilitate future orders, we retain this information for a period of not more than 2 years.
MARKETING OPT-IN: At your option, you may also choose to receive coupons, discounts and special offers from us by email. You may opt-out of these campaigns at any time. instructions for doing so are elsewhere in this document. We neither telemarket nor send advertising by regular mail.
Group “Transaction info (optional)”

At the user’s option, we will collect the following data:

Year of Birth
Month of Birth
This data will be used for the following purposes:

Completion and support of the current activity.
This data will be used by ourselves and our agents‡.

The following explanation is provided for why this data is collected:

IDENTITY QUESTIONNAIRE: To protect against credit card fraud and accelerate fulfillment of your order, we may ask new customers to prove their identity by answering a small number of questions which do not disclose sensitive information but which are unlikely to be answered correctly by credit card thieves. These questions include your month and year of birth. You are not required to answer these questions. We do not retain your answers to these questions.

Cookies

Cookies are a technology which can be used to provide you with tailored information from a Web site. A cookie is an element of data that a Web site can send to your browser, which may then store it on your system. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it.

Our site makes use of cookies. Cookies are used for the following purposes:

Site administration
Completing the user’s current activity
