Privacy Policy

UAB VERO SERVICE (hereinafter referred to as the “Company”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) respects and values your privacy. That's why Company has established this Privacy Policy (referred to as the "Privacy Policy" herein) - so that we can disclose what information Company collects about you and how Company use, store and disclose such information.

 

DEFINITIONS USED

  • Personal data means any information about a natural person who is identified or identifiable (the data subject).
  • The data subject is a natural person, a customer of the Company, including website veroservers.com (hereinafter referred to as the Website) visitors whose personal data are collected by the Company.
  • Data processor means a natural or legal person, a public authority, an agency or other entity that processes personal data on behalf of the data controller.
  • Data processing means any operation or set of operations which is performed on personal data or on sets of personal data, whether or not by automated means, such as collection, recording, organisation, structuring, storage, adaptation or alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination or otherwise making available, alignment or combination, restriction or erasure.
  • Data controller is the Company
  • Cookie is a small piece of information, created when browsing our site and stored on your computer or other end device.

 

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Every time you visit our website your IP address is saved in anonymized form among other. If you register with us, your contact information will be stored. If you order products from us, your address and payment information will also be stored.

The Company collects and further processes personal data, like: name, surname, address, e-mail address, phone number, payment information, that you submit to us yourself by signing up and/or ordering the services on the Website or by ordering services in other ways. Such data is stored during the period of cooperation of the Company and you (during the provision of services) and for 10 (ten) years from the end of this period.

Indirectly provided data (such as IP address or browser’s version) is stored by the Company during the period of cooperation of the Company and you (during the provision of services) and depending on the data, up to one year after the end of this period. These data may be stored for a longer period if there are other legal grounds for such storage period.

 

HOW WE USE THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT?

The Company processes the your personal data for the following purposes of data processing: service purchase (order) processing and administration, identification of the Company’s customers in the Company’s information systems, registering on the Website and logging in to your account, accounting related to the service orders, contacting you regarding the contractual obligations. We may use information for direct marketing purposes, but only with prior consents of the data subjects.

 

HOW WE PROCESS THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT?

We ensure, that your personal data will be processed in lawful, fair and transparent manner. Information is collected for clearly defined and legitimate purposes and is managed in a way to ensure the adequate protection of personal data, including the protection against unauthorized processing or processing of unauthorized data and unintentional loss, destruction or damage by appropriate technical or organizational measures.

 

YOUR RIGHTS

You have the following rights of the data subjects which we will implement upon receiving your request (by email privacy@veroservers.com or by submitting ticket in Website’s client system) after proper authentication: to have an access to your personal data and to be informed of how they are processed; . request to correct any inaccurate personal data relating to you, and request to supplement the incomplete personal data. require the Company to delete personal data relating to you if they are no longer needed to achieve the purposes for which they were collected or otherwise processed.

 

HOW DO WE DISCLOSE PERSONAL DATA WE COLLECT?

Except as disclosed in this Privacy Policy, The Company does not share your personal data with unaffiliated third parties other than as set out in this Privacy Policy. The Company will never disclose your personal information to an unaffiliated third party for the purposes of e-mail or other marketing unless The Company first obtain your consent to do so.

The Company’s employees are given access to your personal data only if it is necessary to fulfil their responsibilities and only with the employee’s obligation to comply with the confidentiality requirement.

The company can provide your personal data to data processors who provide services to us and process your personal data on behalf of the Company and for the benefit of the Company or yourself, only if such provision is necessary for specific service (e.g. domain registration), law enforcement authorities (when there is an undisputable legal basis) or other third parties only with your consent. We only use the data processors who ensure to have the appropriate technical and organizational measures in place, in such a way that the data processing complies with the requirements of the GDPR and ensures the protection of your rights as data subjects.

 

COOKIES

We use cookies to improve the performance of our services and enhance your user experience. By continuing to browse this site you agree to the use of these cookies.

There are three types of cookies we use: Cloudflare's - to provide connection security, session cookie - enables variables to be passed between page loads (cookie expires after browser is closed) and persistent cookies, which enables client area functionality. They are persistant and last for one year or until logout.
