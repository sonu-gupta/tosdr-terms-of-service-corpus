    Scoring method

    The food scoring method used by the App includes the following factors:

    • nutritional balance (60%)
    • presence of food additives (30%)
    • whether the product is organic (10%)

    For nutritional balance, the scoring method is based on Nutri-Score, a nutritional quality information system for food products developed by Sant√© Publique France (www.santepubliquefrance.fr).
