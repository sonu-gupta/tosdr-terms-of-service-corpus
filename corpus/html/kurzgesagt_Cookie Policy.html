In a nutshell – kurzgesagt GmbH (“kurzgesagt”) and our partners use cookies or similar technologies to analyze trends, to administer and track user usage during your visit to our website, online shop or use of our products and to collect information about you, where you access our website or products, and how you use our products and services.

What are cookies and how does kurzgesagt use them?

Cookies are very small text files that are automatically created by the browser and stored on your end device (computer, laptop, tablet, smartphone or similar) when you visit the website. Cookies do not cause any damage to your end device, do not contain viruses, Trojans or other malware.

Cookies in this sense are small files or other technologies with which we can learn how you use our website. This enables us, among other things, to offer you our website, to improve it and to display advertising on the site that is specifically tailored to your interests.

Cookies are very commonly used to make websites and services work and function at higher performance levels and to provide information about our user experience while using or interacting with our websites, products, services and advertisements. However, this does not mean that we obtain immediate knowledge of your identity.

Some cookies only persist for the duration of your web session and expire when you leave the browser. other cookies may persist longer than your web session, including after you leave the browser, for example by remembering you when you return to our website.

In the following, the most common types of cookies are explained for your understanding:

  • Session cookies: While you are active on a website, a session cookie is temporarily stored in the memory of your end device, in which a session identifier is stored, for example to prevent you having to log in again each time you change pages. Session cookies are deleted when you log off or lose their validity as soon as your session has automatically expired.
  • Persistent cookies: Persistent cookies store a file on your mobile device for the period of time specified in the expiry date (storage period). These cookies enable the website to remember your information and settings on your next visit. This leads to faster and more convenient access, as you do not have to change your language settings for our portal again, for example. When the storage period expires (see tables below), the cookie is automatically deleted on your terminal device.
  • Third-party cookies: Third-party cookies are session cookies or persistent cookies that originate from a provider other than the website operator. For example, they may be used to collect information for advertising, custom content and web statistics.

Why are cookies useful?

Cookies serve many different purposes, for example, they enable you to navigate our website efficiently, save your preferred settings and generally improve your visitor experience.

The cookies used on our websites can be divided into the following categories:

  • Necessary cookies: These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of the websites. through them you can navigate our websites and use our functional features. An example of this is the reminder of recent actions (e.g. typed text) when you return to a page within the same session.

These data do not allow any direct conclusion to your person. The legal basis for the collection and processing of personal data is the fulfilment of our contractual obligations (Art. 6 (1) (1) (b) GDPR) or the safeguarding of our legitimate interests, which consist in particular in providing you with the website and its essential functions (Art. 6 (1) (1) (f) GDPR).

  • Statistics cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our websites by providing data about which websites or search terms bring users to our websites, how long they typically stay on our websites or how many subpages they visit on average.

We use this data to improve the content of the websites and to compile statistics for internal market analysis purposes on the individual use of the websites. This data does not allow any direct conclusion to your person. All data collected by us is only processed in aggregated form. The legal basis for the collection and processing of personal data is your explicit permission to do so (Art. 6 (1) (1) (a) GDPR). You can change or withdraw your consent at any time from the cookie declaration on our website.

  • Preference cookies: These cookies allow our websites to remember the choices you make (for example, your username, language, or region of residence) to provide you with a more personalized online experience. They can also allow users to watch videos and interact with social tools such as blogs, chat rooms and forums.

The information collected by these cookies may include personally identifiable information that you have disclosed, such as your user name or profile picture. The legal basis for the collection and processing of personal data is again your explicit permission to do so (Art. 6 (1) (1) (a) GDPR). You can change or withdraw your consent at any time from the cookie declaration on our website.

  • Marketing cookies: These cookies are used to provide content that is more relevant to you and your interests (direct marketing). They can be used to deliver targeted advertising or to limit the frequency with which an advertisement is displayed to you. They also help us to measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns on websites run by kurzgesagt itself and those who are not. We may also use these cookies to store which websites you have visited.

The legal basis for the processing of personal data is for Marketing cookies as well your explicit permission to do so (Art. 6 (1) (1) (a) GDPR). You can change or withdraw your consent at any time from the cookie declaration on our website.
