Such nodes are only accessible by InsanityHost administration and other staff members who require access to carry out their duties (eg. system administrators). Every effort is made to ensure that all public websites use SSL certificates in order to ensure that all data transmitted from your internet browser to our servers is encrypted so that it cannot be intercepted by malicious parties during transmission.
