But if you don’t, no worries. Clario offers a 30-day money-back guarantee! You have 30 days from the day your first payment went through to request a refund.

This 30-day-money-back guarantee applies to Clario consumer products purchased using the following methods:

  • Online purchase via the Clario website
  • Purchase via Google Play.
IMPORTANT

Your refund eligibility may differ if purchased from or billed through a third party (e.g. App Store, a service provider, a reseller, etc.). To review your offer terms and for more information please contact your service provider or reseller directly or refer to App Store's refund policy here.

How can I get a refund?

You can send an email to hello@weareclario.com or talk to us through live chat.

How long does it take to receive my refund?

Once we receive your request, generally, you can expect it to appear in your account after 10 business days.

Can I get a refund after 30 days?

No, a full money-back refund is only available within the first 30 days of your subscription. We’re sorry, but Clario does not provide partial refunds.

However, if you experience technical issues with Clario, you can contact us via email or live chat.

Where can I find out more about your refund policy?

Please contact our Customer Support if you have questions about requesting a refund. We are available 24/7 to give you assistance.

What happens after I have received my refund?

Once you receive your refund, your access to Clario is stopped.
