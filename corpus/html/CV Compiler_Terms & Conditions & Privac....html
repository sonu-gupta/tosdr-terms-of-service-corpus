Service Description

We aim to help you craft a tech resume that will help you get hired faster.

CV Compiler works to find weak points in your resume and immediately suggests how to make improvements.

CV Compiler offers four pricing plans: Free, Advanced Lite, Advanced, and Premium. Depending on the plan you have chosen, you will gain access to some or all of the features below:

  • Automatic resume review(s). Detailed feedback on your resume’s strengths and weaknesses. The number of automatic resume reviews available depends on the plan you select.
  • 15- or 90-day access to your resume review results.
  • Downloadable resume template(s). The number of resumes templates available depends on the plan you select.
  • Cover letter examples. Tips and templates for creating a cover letter.
  • Effective lines. Resume bullet point examples.
  • LinkedIn hacks. Tips to boost your LinkedIn profile
  • Tech Insights. A list of the top tech skills required to land a job at big-name companies.
  • Useful content. A hand-picked list of career-related articles.
  • An in-depth critique (moderation) of your revamped resume. This is the final step.
