Cookies Policy

A cookie is a small unit of data issued by the server of the website that you are viewing and which is registered on the hard disk of your computer or mobile device. The cookie contains a unique code to recognise your browser during your visit to the website or during future visits. Cookies can be placed by the server of the website you visit or by partners with whom this website collaborates. The server of a website can only read the cookies it has placed itself. it does not have access to any other information on your computer or mobile device.

The content of a cookie typically consists of the name of the server that placed the cookie, an expiration date, and a unique encrypted code. Cookies provide generally easier and faster interaction between the visitor and the website (a cookie may retain the user's chosen language, for example). Indeed, they memorise your preferences and allow you to accelerate your subsequent access to the site and facilitate your visits.

In addition, they help you navigate between different parts of the website. Cookies may also be used to make the content of a website more relevant to the visitor and to adapt the website to the personal tastes and needs of the visitor.

The use of cookies requires your prior and explicit consent. You can always return to it later and refuse these cookies and / or delete them at any time, by modifying the settings of your browser.

Our website is using different types of cookies, namely:

-          . Functional cookies:
These cookies are intended to facilitate and analyse the operation of our websites and to make their use more pleasant and personalized. They allow to customize the services by memorising your preferences (choice of a language, currency, location, etc ...), to prevent you from repeating your choices during each visit to the site. They are useful for collecting the information provided in the online forms, compiling statistics (ex. number of unique visitors) and analyzing the use of the site and the popularity of our pages.

-          . Session cookies:
The session cookie is essential for our site to function. It is used to make sure that your experience, whilst on our site, is as seamless as possible as you move from page to page. The cookie is installed automatically when a user connects to the site.

-          . Third-Party Cookies
Third-party cookies are placed by Google (Analytics / DFP)

For example, RTL Group uses Google Analytics and its Content Management System to collect information about how visitors use the website. The cookies collect information anonymously, including the number of visitors to the site, where visitors have come to the site from and the pages they have visited. RTL Group uses this information to compile reports and to improve the site. It is subject to Google’s current cookie policy, which you can find here: http://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/

RTL Group protects the technical information collected by our use of Google Analytics. The data collected will only be used on a need to know basis to resolve technical issues, administer the Site and identify visitor preferences. but in this case, the data will be in non-identifiable form. RTL Group does not use any of this information to identify visitors or users. . However, Google Analytics uses the cookie to identify you as a specific user on whatever other page you use where Google Analytics is activated. Your privacy is ensured. However, you may change your browser settings at any time and choose to access the Websites without accepting Cookies.
