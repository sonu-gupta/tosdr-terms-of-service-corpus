Refund PolicyAs a general principle, we want to make sure you are happy of using our products and pay for them only when you feel they provide value to you. Our refund policy is simple: if you are not satisfied, reach out to us and we'll find a solution.Full refund
  • If you were just charged for your next month or year of service but you meant to cancel, we’re happy to refund that extra charge.

Partial refund
