Information We Collect

You do not have to give us any personally identifiable ("personal") information to browse our Site, but to subscribe to our Services you must create an account and register with us. We do not track you or your internet activities while connected to the VPN Service or VPN Servers.

We collect the following information from registered users:
- Account Information. To create an account, you will need to provide with the following personal information: email address.

- We don’t verify your email address so you have our permission to supply us with non-existing email address. If you do provide us with an email address, real or not, we will store it in a secure way in line with industry standards. We will treat it as personal information and will protect it in line with our privacy policy.

- You will have to provide us with an username and password in order to register an account with us.
- For VAT calculation purposes EU visitors, must select a country for the account. The country choice is not validated by us and it does not have any bearing on your account, it is purely for the purpose of calculate VAT tax if such applies to your purchase.
- In order to be able to process refunds for PayPal, Paymentwall and Coinbase we will collect a transaction ID. If you wish that information to be deleted please refer to “Right to be forgotten” or “How To Modify Your Personal Information” sections below.
- If you purchase "Dedicated IP" add-on we will keep a record of which username purchased which dedicated IP. As a dedicated IP owner you can request we remove this information. We also remove Dedicated IP ownership information if we re-assign the dedicated IP to another customer if you let it expire.

Payments
To register an account with us you will have to place a payment which will be processed by one of the following payment processors externally - PayPal.com, Paymentwall.com, Coinbase.com.

All PayPal transactions are subject to the PayPal Privacy Policy.

Please refer to their privacy policies if you agree to pay via any of them.

No Logs

We do not monitor, record or store logs for any single customer's VPN activity. We do not monitor, record or store any login dates, timestamps, incoming and outgoing IP addresses, bandwidth statistics or any other identifiable data of any VPN users using our VPN servers. We do not log or track any DNS requests sent to our DNS servers.

Site Visitors

We collect certain information automatically from visitors to our Site via Cookies. For more information refer to our official Cookie Policy here: Cookie Policy. We do not track user activities outside of our Site, nor do we track the browsing activities of users who are logged into our VPN service via cookies or in any other way.
