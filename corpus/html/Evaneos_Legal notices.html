Cookies

EVANEOS may need to generate a cookie on the user's computer. A cookie does not allow EVANEOS to identify it, however, it does record information relating to browsing on www.evaneos.net conducted from the user's computer (pages visited by the user, date and time of consultation, etc.) which EVANEOS will be able to read on the user’s subsequent visits to the website. This information may be kept on the user's computer for 1 (one) year. EVANEOS informs the user that they may prevent the generation of "cookies" by configuring their browser in the following manner:

1.   . For Mozilla Firefox:

2.   . Go to the "tools" menu, then "options"

3.   . Click on the "private life" icon

4.   . Go to the “cookie” menu and select the relevant options

1.   . For Microsoft Internet Explorer 6.0:

2.   . Open the “Tools” menu, then “Internet Options”

3.   . Click on “Confidentiality”

4.   . Select the level of confidentiality using the cursor

1.   . For Microsoft Internet Explorer 5:

2.   . Open the “Tools” menu, then “Internet Options”

3.   . Click on "Security"

4.   . Select "Internet" then "CustomLevel"

5.   . Go to the “cookie” menu and select the relevant options

1.   . For Netscape 6.X and 7. X:

2.   . Select "Edit >Preferences"

3.   . Confidentiality and Security

4.   . Cookies

1.   . For Opera 6.0 and above:
