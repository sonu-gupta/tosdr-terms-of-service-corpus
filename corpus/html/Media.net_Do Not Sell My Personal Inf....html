Opt-Out

Once you have opted out, Media.net will no longer collect any information from your device and as a result, our partners will not be able to serve behaviorally targeted advertisements.

  • Since we identify you based on your browser, you will have to 'opt out' on all browsers you use.
Mobile Apps

For mobile applications, your device gives you the ability to opt-out from behaviorally targeted advertisements. You may opt-out of behavioral targeting by updating your preferences, "Opt out of Interest-Based Ads" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android devices or "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS devices. Further details on how to change your preferences - http://www.networkadvertising.org/mobile-choice

Additionally, you may change the preference of sharing the location information by apps from your device by changing location preferences on your device.

Your Data Privacy

When you visit any website, it may store or retrieve information on your browser, mostly in the form of cookies. This information might be about you, your preferences or your device and is mostly used to make the site work as you expect it to. The information does not usually directly identify you, but it can give you a more personalized web experience. Because we respect your right to privacy, you can choose not to allow some types of cookies. Click on the different category headings to find out more and change our default settings. However, blocking some types of cookies may impact your experience of the site and the services we are able to offer.
