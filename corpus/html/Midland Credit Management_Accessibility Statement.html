Midland Credit Management, Inc. (“MCM”) is committed to providing an accessible experience to our consumers and the public, regardless of disability status. Please contact our Consumer Resolution Account Managers at (800) 265-8825 ext. 23124 for English or ext. 58600 for Spanish to learn more about accessibility support services at MCM. MCM welcomes relay calls.

Reasonable Accommodations

Individuals who need a reasonable accommodation to access MCM’s services should call us at (800) 265-8825 ext. 23124 for English or ext. 58600 for Spanish to provide information about the nature of the requested accommodation. Requesters should include contact information such as an email address or telephone number at which they can be reached. Depending on the nature of the request, MCM may need sufficient notice to provide a reasonable accommodation.

Online Accessibility

MCM strives to provide an accessible digital experience for our consumers. In the event that a user with a disability experiences accessibility issues with our website, please notify us by calling us at (800) 265-8825 ext. 23124 for English or ext. 58600 for Spanish. In your communication to us, please specify the nature of the accessibility difficulty, including the web address that may have presented an accessibility challenge.

Third-Party Websites

MCM’s website contains links to webpages hosted by third parties. MCM does not make representations with regard to the accessibility of third-party websites and is not able to remediate accessibility barriers on such websites.

Feedback

We are always working to ensure that our services are accessible to all consumers and the public, including individuals with disabilities.
