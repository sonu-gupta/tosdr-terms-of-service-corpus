Antisip Privacy Policy

This privacy policy will explain how our organization uses the personal data we collect from you when you use our website.

What data do we collect

Antisip SARL collects the following data:

  • Personal identification information (Name, email address, phone number, postal address)
  • We save the IP address used to register to the SIP service.
  • We maintain call history when you use the SIP service.
How do we collect your data?

You directly provide Antisip SARL with most of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:

  • Register online to use our services.
  • Use or view our website via your browser’s cookies.
  • Use the sip services (For the purpose of call history only)

Antisip SARL may also receive your data indirectly from the following sources:

  • None
How will we use your data?

Antisip SARL collects your data so that we can:

  • Manage your account.
  • Email you about our services.
  • Email you the received voicemails.

We do not share your data with anyone.

How do we store your data?

Antisip SARL securely stores your data on a private server hosted by ovh and only access it in a secure way.

Antisip SARL will keep your history data for a year. Once this time period has expired, we will delete your data.

Marketing

Antisip SARL will not use your personal data for marketing.

If you have agreed to receive marketing, you may always opt out at a later date.

You have the right at any time to stop Antisip SARL from contacting you for marketing purposes or giving your data to other members of the Antisip SARL Group.

If you no longer wish to be contacted for marketing purposes, please contact us.

What are your data protection rights?

Antisip SARL would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access – You have the right to request Antisip SARL for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.

The right to rectification – You have the right to request that Antisip SARL correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request Antisip SARL to complete the information you believe is incomplete.

The right to erasure – You have the right to request that Antisip SARL erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that Antisip SARL restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to Antisip SARL’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to data portability – You have the right to request that Antisip SARL transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.
