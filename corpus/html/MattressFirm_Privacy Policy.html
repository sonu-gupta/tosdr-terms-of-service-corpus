  • Clickstream Data. . We collect clickstream data, which provides a non-personalized record of your activity on the internet using the items and webpages that you click on. . We use this data to analyze how you use our websites.
  • Cookies. . We collect cookies, which are small text files that may be stored on your device when you use our websites.  The cookies we use may contain information – such as a unique user ID – or collect information, such as your email address if you create an account with us on any of our websites.  Cookies allow us to recognize your device when you return to our website. They also help us to understand how you use our website and to provide you with advertising and content that we think may be of interest to you.
  • Pixel Tags. . A pixel tag (also known as a web beacon) is a small string of code that represents a clear graphic image. . We use pixel us to collect non-personalized information about your activity on our website (e.g., time, date, duration, and number of page views), so that we can improve our website functioning and to provide you with advertising and content that we think may be of interest to you.

Your California Privacy Rights If you are a California resident, the below information applies to you. Certain terms used in this section have the meanings given to them under the California Consumer Privacy Act (“CCPA”).

Shine the Light

Pursuant to Section 1798.83 of the California Civil Code, if you have an established business relationship with a business, you have the right to request from that business, certain information with respect to the types of personal information that business shares with third parties for those third parties' direct marketing purposes and the identities of the third parties with whom that business has shared such information during the immediately preceding calendar year. Submit your request here.

Please note that under California law, businesses are only required to respond to a Shine the Light request once during any calendar year.

California Consumer Privacy Act

Pursuant to Sections 1798.110 and 1798.115 of the California Civil Code, you have the right to request that a business disclose to you the following:

(1) The categories of personal information it has collected about you;

(2) The categories of sources from which the personal information is collected;

(3) The business or commercial purpose for collecting, selling or sharing personal information;

(4) The categories of third parties to whom the business discloses personal information;

(5) The specific pieces of personal information it has collected about you;

(6) The categories of personal information that the business sold or shared about you, if any, and the categories of third parties to whom the personal information was sold or shared, by category or categories of personal information for each category of third party to whom the personal information was sold or shared. and

(7) The categories of personal information that the business disclosed about you for a business purpose, if any, and the categories of persons to whom your personal information was disclosed for a business purpose.

Pursuant to Section 1798.105 of the California Civil Code, you have the right to request that a business delete any personal information about you which the business has collected from you.

Pursuant to Section 1798.106 of the California Code, you have the right to request that a business that maintains inaccurate personal information about you, correct such inaccurate information.

Pursuant to Section 1798.121 of the California Code, you have the right to request that a business that collects sensitive personal information about you to limit its use of your sensitive personal information to that which is necessary to perform the services or provide the goods requested by you.

To exercise any of these rights, you may submit your request online, or send a written request to: “Mattress Firm, 10201 S. Main Street, Houston, TX 77025, Attn: Legal Department”. If submitting a written request, please include your name, mailing address, telephone number and email address.
