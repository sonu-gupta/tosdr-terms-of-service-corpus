Collecting Your Personal Information
This Online Privacy Notice covers the collection and use of personal information on the site. Personal Information is data that reasonably can be used to identify or describe an individual. Computer Solution Partners collects personal information online when:

  • you register to become a member of eTherapyDocs or another Computer Solution Partners service (such as the Computer Solution Partners Partner Site)
  • you submit questions or comments to us
  • you request information or materials
  • you request warranty or post-warranty service and support for a eTherapyDocs product
  • you register products online
  • you participate in online surveys
  • you register to become a subscriber to a eTherapyDocs Service
  • you participate in online promotions, premiums, sweepstakes, contests, or other registrations
  • you request information be sent to your colleagues or customers

Using Your Information
eTherapyDocs uses the personal information you provide to:

  • register you in programs
  • create and maintain accounts
  • process, fulfill, and follow up on orders
  • answer your email
  • send information you request
  • communicate with regard to your account or system status, send surveys, and register products
  • send information to colleagues on your behalf

eTherapyDocs will not sell, rent, or trade the personal information you provide online without providing notice or obtaining your consent. We may also collect and record information about what you viewed on our website. What are “cookies” and how does eTherapyDocs use them?
Cookies are small pieces of information stored by your browser on your computer’s hard drive, at a website’s request. eTherapyDocs cookies do not contain any personal information and are used for these reasons:

  1. cookies allow us to keep track of the pictures you upload and download;
  2. cookies allow us to remember you when you login to the places on our site which require Membership;
  3. to remember your country and language preferences;
  4. to help us understand the size of our audience and traffic patterns. to manage and present site information;
  5. to deliver information specific to your interests. and
  6. to manage site information and pictures displayed on your computer.

Do I have to have a cookie set to use this site?
eTherapydocs can be accessed with cookies disabled. however, your visit will be significantly enhanced if cookies are enabled. Some parts of the site will require cookies to be enabled.
You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it. Please click on the “Help” menu of your browser for information on setting cookie preferences.
