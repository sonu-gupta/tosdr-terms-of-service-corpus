Becoming part of the honeygain network means that the user willingly shares his internet traffic with honeygain and Clients. Clients are not aware which specific users’ internet traffic they are using, nor users know which Client uses their internet traffic. Normally, the Client requests for internet traffic that could be shared from a specific country (i.e. Brazil) or region (i.e. South America) and honeygain can either grant or deny such requests depending on the quantity, location and / or honeygain functionality options of users that participate in honeygain’s network at the time.
