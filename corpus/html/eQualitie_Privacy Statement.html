Your personal data is protected by PIPEDA. You retain full rights of your personal data and may contact us to request what personal data we hold on your behalf, or to delete it.

If you have submitted personal data on this site, you can request its deletion by writing to us using the contact form. This does not include any data we are obliged to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes.
