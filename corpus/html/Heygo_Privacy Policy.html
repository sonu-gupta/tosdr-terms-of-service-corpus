Information we collect

When you register for an account with us, you may provide us with your personal details, including your email address, name and your chosen password. Once you have registered you can edit your personal details in your account. If you log in with Google, Facebook or another social media account, those sites may give us access to your limited personal details (including your email address and account ID) in order to verify if you are logged in with their site. Once you are logged in to our Website, your use of the Website and your data will be handled in accordance with this Privacy Policy and the Cookie Policy. We are not responsible for any use of your personal data by Google, Facebook or any other social media site.

The information we collect may include:

  • your behavior on our Website, including but not limited to the terms you search for, videos you watch and the messages you send on the Website’s chat function;
  • views and interactions with content and ads, including those shown on third party websites.
  • voice and audio information when you use audio features.
  • purchase activity;
  • the way you access our digital services, including operating system, IP address (which is helpful to save your timezone preferences), online identifier details.
  • social preferences, interests and activities.
  • people with whom you communicate or share content with. and
  • activity on third-party sites and apps that use our services.

If you book, or request to book, a pre-paid tour through the Website, you may provide us with additional personal information, such as your name, email address, gender, city, country, preferred language(s), group size, interests, ages and further details about you or your group members. With your consent, these will be shared with the Host you are requesting to book with.

If you express an interest in partnering with us as a Host, then we may collect additional personal information, such as your city, country, phone number, information about you or your guiding experience and companies worked for, and personal photos you choose to display.

When you make a purchase or tip you will be required to supply payment card details. We use Stripe as a third party payment provider to manage online card payment transactions. Payment card information is collected over a secure online payment gateway provided by Stripe and processed in accordance with their privacy policy, which can be found here.

All payment transactions processed are encrypted. Your payment card information is used solely to process payments for any bookings or tips made. We retain no record of your payment card details.

When you communicate with Heygo we collect information about your communication and any additional information you choose to provide. You may also send us information when you fill in a form, update or add information to your Heygo account, respond to surveys, participate in promotions, communicate with our team, share your experiences with us or use other features of the Website.

How we use the information we collect

We use the information we collect to:

  • provide and administer the Website and related services and manage internal operations, including troubleshooting, data analysis, research, testing, statistical and survey purposes, and to develop the Website and our products and services.
  • develop new services based on the information we collect in existing services.
  • customise our services to you, including providing recommendations, personalised content, ads and search results.
  • provide customers who have agreed to receive marketing communications, with relevant and timely offers by email.
  • provide a user with a friendly online search experience. This includes the use of website analytics tools as described further in our Cookie Policy.
  • identify the location of visitors, to block disruptive or fraudulent use, and to establish the number of visits from different countries. and
  • communicate with you directly. For example, we may send you notifications when you have upcoming tours or new tours are being released.

In this event, you will need to contact them to withdraw your consent to their use of your personal data.
