How we use cookies

  1. Like many online services providers, we also use cookies to collect additional website usage data to operate and improve our Services.   2. Via cookies, we may also collect certain of your personal data and then process it for the purposes set out below, where we also provide you with information to help you to decide whether you would like to leave cookies turned on or if you would prefer to disable them.

  3. Our cookies do not, by themselves, contain information that directly identifies you, and we do not combine the general information collected through cookies with other such information to tell us who you are. We use cookies for the following purposes:

    1. Our Website uses cookies to remember who you are, i.e. to realize if you are logged in to your user account. The use of such cookies is necessary to provide you, as a registered user, with access to your user account and to be able to utilize the features of the Services to their fullest potential. Our Services cannot function properly without these technical cookies.

    2. Our Website also uses Google Analytics cookies for the purpose of the creation of statistical reports about general usage by our users and web traffic routing on our Website. We use the information contained in these reports to understand how you interact with the Website and our Services so that we can effectively manage them and improve them. More information about Google Analytics cookies can be found here. The use of cookies for analytical purposes is based on the consent given by you to us when you enter our Website if the cookies are enabled in your browser.

  4. Most Internet browsers automatically accept cookies. By personalizing the options, you can instruct your browser to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting cookies from the websites you visit. Please note that if you delete, or choose not to accept, technical cookies from the Services (other than Google Analytics cookies), you will not be able to log into your account.
