The enrollment and billing privacy statement provides our customers with a detailed look at how personal data is collected and processed when enrolling for a NortonLifeLock service. For more information about our data processing practices and any rights you may have, please see our Global Privacy Statement.

Norton Enrollment Process

The Norton Enrollment process applies to all customers who are purchasing their Norton products and standalone features through the Norton eStore or by calling into a Norton contact center. This information is only collected once and is used to set up the Norton Account. Additional information may be collected at the discretion of the customer in order to maintain the account during the subscription to the product.

 . Categories of Data and Personal Data Collected Purpose of Processing Your Personal Data Data You Provide: User Data may include:
  • Email
  • Norton account password
  • Name (if given)
  • Country (if given)
  • Phone number (if given)
  • National ID (region specific, if outside the United States)
  • Physical address
  • Tax ID (region specific, if outside the United States)
User Data: This data is processed to authenticate, administer, and maintain the account and product subscription as well as to communicate with you.
Data We Collect/Access: User Data may include:
  • IP address
  • Cookies from websites
  • Device ID
Administrative data including:
  • Norton account ID
User Data: This data is processed for the purposes of delivering the content in accordance with your device(s), determining appropriate language settings for communicating with you, and monitoring website activity from you (cookies).

Administrative Data: This data is used and generated internally at NortonLifeLock for the purpose of tracking account activity between internal company systems, applications, and architecture. Data We Collect/Access from Third Parties: NortonLifeLock does not collect any other data for enrollment processes from any third parties. NortonLifeLock does not collect any other data for enrollment processes from any third parties. Norton Billings Process

The Norton Billing process applies to all customers who have purchased their Norton products and/or standalone features through the Norton eStore or by calling into a Norton contact center. This information is used to bill the customers for their services and products on a regular interval. For products purchased through other methods, such as Norton 360 App for Apple and Android, this information will not apply.

 . Categories of Data and Personal Data Collected Purpose of Processing Your Personal Data Data You Provide:

User Data may include:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Norton account password
  • Phone number (if given)
  • National ID (region specific, if outside the United States)
  • Billing address
  • Payment information (depending on method can include Credit/Debit Card Information or PayPal account)
  • VAT/Tax ID (region specific, if outside the United States)
User Data: This data is processed to bill the account for products and features purchased. Data We Collect/Access:

User Data may include:

  • IP address
  • Cookies from websites

Administrative Date may include:

  • Wallet ID
  • Auto bill ID
  • Payment transaction ID
  • Browser details
User Data: This data is processed for the purposes of delivering the content in accordance with your device(s), determining appropriate language settings for communicating with you, troubleshooting issues, generating appropriate diagnostics reports, and monitoring website activity from the User (cookies).

Administrative Data: This data is used and generated internally at NortonLifeLock for the purpose of tracking account activity between internal company systems, applications, and architecture. This data is also processed for the purpose of delivering the product in accordance with your device(s) as well as for trade compliance and fraud check.
Data We Collect/Access from Third Parties:

User Data may include:

  • Payment information (depending on method can include Credit/Debit Card Information or PayPal account)
  • Billing address

Security Data may include:

  • Device fingerprint ID from third party
User Data: This data is processed by third parties to bill the account for products and features purchased and to authenticate the payment information used.

Security Data: This data is processed by third parties and NortonLifeLock to validate that an authenticated payment transaction occurred, and the payment card and account are appropriate for billing.
