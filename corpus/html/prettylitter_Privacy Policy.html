When you access or use our Service, we automatically collect information about you, including:

  • Information Collected by Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies: We and our service providers use various technologies to collect information, including cookies and web beacons. . Cookies are small data files stored on your hard drive or in device memory that help us improve our Service and your experience, see which areas of our Service are popular and count visits. . For more information about cookies, and how to disable them, please see "Your Choices" below.

Please note that you will need to opt out again if you visit one of our Services from a different device or browser of if you clear your cookies.
