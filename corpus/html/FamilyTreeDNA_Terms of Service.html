  19. General Practices Regarding Use and Storage

    FamilyTreeDNA's sample collection kits include two vials, each labeled with a barcode associating the vials with the order. Barcodes are scanned at check-in, triggering a notification email to the kit owner of receipt. A single vial is sent to our federally compliant CAP/CLIA certified lab for DNA extraction where the DNA is stored in our robotic freezer at -20 C, ready to be processed for the ordered test(s). The remaining DNA is returned to the freezer and stored for any future test(s) ordered by the kit owner. The second vial is stored in a secured room at room temperature for up to 25 years and is used only in the event that your DNA from the first vial does not provide sufficient quantity or quality for the tests the kit owner ordered.

    Our customers' trust and privacy are important to us, therefore we do not share or transfer stored samples or extracted DNA with third parties.

    You acknowledge and agree that FamilyTreeDNA has no responsibility or liability for the failure to store or the deletion of any content maintained or transmitted by the Services. or for the loss of DNA samples or Genetic Information due to malfunction or destruction of data servers or other catastrophic events.
