    We believe in the quality of our products but we care about the experience of our customers more. 


    If any of the products you’ve purchased from Rampow fail to function as promised, please use the Warranty Claim system to get the quickest resolution. We will come back to you ASAP. 


    Before everything, there’s something you should know. 


    Rampow 12-Month Warranty


    What’s 12-month Warranty? 
    • Rampow provides an 12-month Warranty from the date of purchase for all Rampow® products sold by Rampow.


    • Rampow warrants these products against defects in materials and workmanship under normal use for a period of 12 months, as applicable, from the date of purchase. 


    • If a valid claim is made during the applicable period, Rampow, at its option, will either
    1. replace the product
    2. exchange the product with a product of equal value
    3. refund the order.


    • A replacement product assumes the remaining warranty of the original product or 90 days from the date of replacement, whichever is greater. 


    Rampow Conditional Lifetime Warranty


    What’s Conditional Lifetime Warranty? 
    • For the benefits of our customers, we extend the 12-month Warranty to Conditional Lifetime Warranty for customers who have registered their products on our website.


    • To extend and validate Conditional Lifetime Warranty for your purchases, you’ll have to register your product with a valid email address and Order ID. Once registered, you’ll receive a confirmation. If you fail to receive anything, please kindly check the junk mailbox or contact us.


    • For purchases under one email address, all orders will be covered by this Conditional Lifetime Warranty once the email address is registered. There’s no need to register again for multiple purchases.


    • Rampow warrants these products against defects in materials and workmanship under normal use. 

    What does the Warranty cover? 


    • All Rampow products sold by Amazon Seller Rampow.
    • All Rampow products sold by AliExpress Seller Rampow.

    Warranty Exclusions &. Limitations


    • Though we care about the happiness of our customers, the following cases WILL NOT be covered by our Warranty:
    1. Products purchased from unauthorized resellers;
    2. Purchases without valid proof of purchase; 
    3. Any defects or damages caused by unreasonable use, abuse, exposure to excessive heat, liquids or other external causes;
    4. Lost or stolen products;
    5. Product that has been refunded. 

    Valid proof of purchase


    What’s a valid proof of purchase?
    • Acceptable proof of purchase is an accurate Amazon/AliExpress Order Number and matching email address when you placed that order. A screen capture of your Amazon/AliExpress order summary is also valid. The important information that needs to be included are as follows:
    1. Product Puchased
    2. Date
    3. Seller
    4. Amazon/AliExpress Order Number
    5. Email Address


    Why you require proof of purchase?
    • We require a valid proof of purchase to ensure that the Rampow product was purchase directly from Amazon/AliExpress Seller Rampow. If you purchased directly from us, we may be able to locate your order in our system. Rampow cannot guarantee any type of warranty or support on products acquired through unauthorized channels.

    How do I make a claim? 


    • To make a warranty claim for defective products, please use our Warranty Claims system for the quickest resolution. 


    How Do I Contact You Directly?


    • If you have any questions or concerns, please drop us a message. Hope you enjoy every purchase with Rampow.
