    Latest blog post Free PDF Crochet Pattern: Create an Amigurumi Doll

    Download this pattern and bring your own charming character to life alongside a professional designer Amigurumis are crocheted characters full of emotion and personality who have the power to convey the feelings of whoever knits them–that’s what really makes them come alive. This free crochet pattern, made by amigurumi designer Elisa Ems-Domenig (@lulu_loves_the_moon), is designed to help you create a characterful doll of your own. Elisa sells crochet patterns and figures via her brand Lulu loves the moon. Inspired by her home near the Alps, she creates designs with neutral tones from nature and has had her work featured in crochet books and magazines internationally. In this article, Elisa shares a free PDF Crochet Pattern to create an Amigurumi Doll. Learn step by step how to knit these adorable characters with this practical resource.

Cookies

A cookie is a file that is downloaded to your terminal/device (PC or mobile) with the purpose of storing data that may be updated and recovered by the entity that installs them. Our Platform uses "cookies" to collect information about its operation and its users' experience.

We do not store sensible personally identifiable information such as your address, passwords, or credit/debit card information in the cookies we use.

Purpose

Cookies can track information about your visits to our Platform, their frequency and length, the pages viewed and the time spent in our Platform. We may use cookies to:

  • Identify problems to improve our Platform

  • Enable you to better navigate our Platform

  • Set your apart from other users

We also ask for your consent to use cookies to allow us to obtain more information about your preferences and customize our Platform to meet your interests.

Types of Cookies

Our own cookies

They are sent to your device and managed exclusively by us in order to improve the performance of our Platform. These cookies remain in your browser, allowing us to identify you as a returning user, controlling the user login session, remembering cookies configuration, recognize you as a user in case of managing identification, and customize your content to offer you information according to your preferences (individualization cookies).

  • Technical Cookies: They allow you to navigate the Platform and use its services such as controlling data communication, make a purchase, store content, personalize language and currency, format check, share content through social media according to user preferences, speed-up page loading, etc., such as Custora, Olark, Facebook, CloudFare, Visual Website Optimizer.
