The term personal data encompasses all information about an identifiable, natural person, including, but not limited to, first and last name, house- and work address, credit card data, e-mail address and other contact details. For the sake of this privacy policy, we will consider IP-addresses (internet-protocol) personal identifiable information as well.

Legal basis for the use of personal data

Goose processes your personal data exclusively on the basis of the legal grounds referred to hereafter as intended in article 6 of the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Creating/executing an agreement with the person concerned
  • Permission of the person concerned – Please note: you can withdraw this permission at any time
  • Legal requirements that Goose is obliged to follow
  • Justified (commercial) interest of Goose

Security Measures

Goose ensures that – taking the level of the technology into account – appropriate technical and organizational security measures are taken to guarantee a level of security appropriate to the risks.

Disclosing of personal data to third parties

Goose does not sell, rent or trade your personal data to third parties. Goose shares your personal data only insofar as it is necessary for providing the services while taking the purposes mentioned above into account. If Goose employs the services of a party who processes your personal data in Goose’s name and at Goose’s behest, a processor’s agreement will be made with that party.

Retention Policy

Your personal data will not be stored any longer than is necessary for the purposes described in this privacy policy.

Your privacy rights

You have numerous privacy rights. A request to insight, correction, limitation, protest and transferal of data, removal of your personal data or withdrawal of permission granted earlier can all be made via our contact form. Within 4 weeks of receiving your request, you will receive word from us. We may ask you – to be certain that you were the one to file the request – to send a copy of your ID along as well. In this copy, blacken out your passport photograph, MRZ, passport number and social security number. If you are displeased with our treatment of your personal data, you can file a complaint with the Data Protection Authority.

II. Privacy on the website goosevpn.com

Processing personal data – website

Should you be visiting our website, Goose processes your personal data in the following cases:

  • Access – you have access to content on the website goosevpn.com without registering and without providing any personal identifiable information;
  • Contact – When you contact our online helpdesk, the helpdesk employee will be able to see your country of origin and the local IP-address. This data will not be stored. You are not required to provide our helpdesk employee your name, e-mail address or other personal information. When you contact our helpdesk by e-mail, we will store your e-mail address.
  • Surveys – We do not collect any of our personal data when you fill out an online survey.

Goose does not intend to process personal data or processing data on visitors to the website that are younger than 16 years of age. For this reason, Goose requests that this data not be sent.

Use of your personal data – website

Your personal data can be used for the following purposes:

  • Chat-helpdesk: through personal data that you have provided us when using the online chat-helpdesk, we can send answers directly to your e-mail address, should the chat-session be interrupted. We do not add your e-mail address to our mailing list automatically.
  • Mail-helpdesk: through personal data that you have provided us when contacting our e-mail helpdesk, we can send the answers to your questions directly to your e-mail address. We do not add your e-mail address to our mailing list automatically.
  • Mailings: Should you give us permission to, Goose can, using the e-mail address you have provided, keep you informed of the most recent service information, software-updates, offers and promotions and improve our service, website and advertising campaigns. If you do not wish to remain part of our mailing list, you can unsubscribe while subscribing or do so later, by unsubscribing using the footer in one of our e-mails.

Cookies and other technologies – website

The website, e-mails and advertisements of Goose may employ the use of ‘cookies’ and other technologies such as pixeltags and web beacons. Using these technologies, Goose can gain better insight into user behavior, knowledge of which parts of our website have been visited and the effectivity of advertisements and internet searches can be facilitated and measured. All information that is collected with cookies and other technologies, is stored and treated as personally non-identifiable data. Goose also makes use of cookies and other technologies to store information when you visit our website. Our goal in these instances is the improvement of your experience with Goose. Should you wish to deactivate cookies, go to the security settings of your browser and turn cookies off. On a mobile device, go to the section marked cookies at settings or preferences. With specific browsers, contact your provider on how to disable cookies. Keep in mind that certain functions of Goose’s website are not available when cookies are turned off. In certain e-mails, we make use of a ‘click-through URL’, that refers you to content on the website goosevpn.com. When customers click on a URL such as this, it is possible that they are first routed through a separate GOOSE-webserver before they reach designated page. We collect this clickthrough-data to ascertain the interest in certain subjects as well as the effectiveness of our customer communication. If you would rather that this clickthrough data is not collected in this manner, do not click on textual or graphic links in e-mail messages. Pixeltags allow us to send e-mails in a format that is legible to our customers. We can use this information to limit or eliminate messages for our customers.

Security Measures – Website

The online cash registers, my account-pages and the helpdesk portal of Goose use SSL-encryption (Secure Sockets Layer) on all webpages where personal data is collected. To make use of an SSL-encryption of personal data that is transferred through the internet to these pages, you will have to use an SSL-browser. When you use Goose’s online chat-helpdesk, the personal information that you share will be visible to the helpdesk employee and can be read and/or used by him/her. You yourself are responsible for the personal data (such as your name and e-mail address) that you provide in such instances.

III. Privacy on GOOSE VPN’s secure VPN server network

Using Goose’s services encompasses the sending of data through the internet via servers and IP-addresses from the server network of Goose by means of the VPN software application: GOOSE VPN. The server network of Goose is property of and is administrated and monitored by Goose BV in Rotterdam, the Netherlands. GOOSE VPN is developed, maintained and administrated by Goose BV in Rotterdam, the Netherlands.

Processing personal data – VPN-services

When you use GOOSE VPN, Goose processes personal data in the following instances:

  • When you register for a free trial or a paid service of GOOSE VPN, we save the e-mail address that you provide us. This data, combined with your account data as a user of GOOSE VPN, is stored in our CMS-system on one of our secure servers;
  • We do not register your IP-address when you sign up or log into your GOOSE account.
  • When you sign up, we register your IP-address one time only;
  • When you use GOOSE VPN, Goose collects information on the actual bandwidth consumption per user account.
  • Payment details? –
  • When logging onto the website, Goose stores the last-used IP-address. However, this does not apply to the application or other actions, Goose does not save the IP-address (internet protocol) in a cache memory, nor does it collect or save this;
  • Goose does not save any data concerning the servers or the IP-address on the server network of Goose when a user is connected on a certain time or in general, in a cache memory nor does it collect it or store it;
  • Goose does not save any registrations of internet activities of users (for example, websites visited, DNS-search results, e-mails sent and received, usernames or entered passwords, etc.) in a cache memory nor does it collect this, or save this, or aggregates this, or use this through the network servers of GOOSE VPN.

Use of personal data – VPN-services

Your personal data can be used for the following purposes:

  • The e-mail address that you provide us when you sign up for a free trial or a paid service of GOOSE VPN is used to send you the data pertaining to your user account. The e-mail address that you provide us can also be used to send you important notifications such as communication about purchases or changes to our terms of use and policy. Because this information is important to you as a user of GOOSE VPN, you will not be able to unsubscribe from these mailings.
  • Goose does not censor nor monitor the internet activity of users;
  • Goose does not censor nor monitor or creates back-ups of users’ e-mails

Cookies and other technologies – VPN-services

Goose does not use any cookies or other technologies on the servers of Goose. Not a single outgoing server of the server network of Goose contains information on the server, on the network or of users. The operating system of the servers is turned off except for modules that are necessary for the transferal of internet traffic.

Security Measures – VPN-services

All servers of Goose are secured with Linux hardening, firewalls and software is installed against spam, viruses and DDOS-attacks to prevent third-parties from carrying out malicious attacks on the server network of Goose. We maintain our servers and networks to offer as much privacy as possible. Goose does its utmost to protect information about bandwidth use of users and all related data from loss, theft or unauthorized access, abuse, disclosure, alteration and destruction. This includes, but is not limited to, the beneficial application of bandwidth use of users between the server network of Goose and the network maintenance system of Goose.

Questions about privacy

Should you have questions or comments on Goose’s privacy policy, please contact us through the contact form. GOOSE VPN can actualize the privacy policy at set times. If we thoroughly adjust the policy, a message will be placed on our website along with the updates to our privacy policy.
