  1. Adjusting your privacy and advertising settings on your mobile device (where your mobile device permits this) to control whether you receive interest-based targeted advertising. Learn more at https://www.networkadvertising.org/mobile-choices

We do not recommend turning all cookies off when using our websites. We also use a browser-based cookie to remember your cookie preferences. If you use a different device, computer profile or browser you will have to tell us your preferences again. If you want to reduce your cookie settings at any time – for example, if you accept all cookies, but later decide you don’t want a certain type of cookie – you’ll need to use your browser settings to remove any third party cookies dropped on your previous visit. Your Rights

To make an access or deletion request, please submit your request at 1-800-442-4059 or https://privacy.footlocker-inc.com/consumer/index  

Before we can process your request, we will ask you a series of questions in order to attempt to verify your identity. If you use an agent to make a deletion or access request, we may ask the agent to verify their own identity and directly confirm with you that you provided the authorized agent permission to submit the request. If you use an authorized agent to submit a request to opt-out of sales of personal information, we may deny a request from an authorized agent that does not submit proof that they have been authorized by you.

Access Request. You may make up to two (2) requests per year for information as to:

  • The categories of personal information that we have collected and sold about you, or shared for a business purpose with our service providers;
  • The sources from which we collected such information;
  • The third parties with whom we have shared that personal information and to whom we have sold that personal information;
  • The business or commercial purpose for collecting or selling that personal information. and
  • The specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.

We will endeavor to respond to your request within forty-five (45) days of receiving your request or notify you that we need additional time to process your information.

Deletion Request. You may request that we delete the personal information that we have collected from or about you. Note that there are some reasons when we will not be able to fully address your request, such as if we need to complete a transaction for you, to detect and protect against fraudulent and illegal activity, to maintain for internal purposes, or to comply with a legal obligation or to exercise our rights.

Opt-Out Request. As noted above, we share Personal Information with certain third parties.  The potential “sales” we make (as defined by California law) are in connection with the collection (by us or by certain advertising providers) and sharing of certain information to enable personalized ads on our Site, App, and on other sites that you visit. If you are a California resident, the CCPA gives you the right to tell us not to “sell” your personal information to third parties. This means that, if you opt-out, going forward, we will not share your information with certain third parties to use for their own commercial purposes unless you later direct us to do so.

You or your authorized agent may opt-out by clicking on the Do Not Sell My Personal Information link on our website footer or by going to https://privacy.footlocker-inc.com/consumer/donotsell and following the instructions or by calling 1-800-442-4059. For more information on managing cookies used for advertising purposes, see the Section above entitled Use and Management of Cookies and Interest Based Advertising.

Please note that if, after opting out, you submit a deletion request or change the Personal Information that you share with us, you may be required to submit a subsequent opt-out request.
