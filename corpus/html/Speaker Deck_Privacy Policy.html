Cookies

A cookie is a small amount of data, which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a web site’s computers and stored on your computer’s hard drive.

Speaker Deck uses cookies to record current session information, our cookies generally last two weeks. You are required to log-in to your Speaker Deck account after a certain period of time has elapsed to protect you against others accidentally accessing the content of your account.
