Content Rating Levels You must ensure that your posts are tagged properly.

Users can select through their account settings, various "Content Rating Levels", which algorithmically determines what content may be most suitable and interesting for that user.
Below is a description of each rating level:

    This is typically content that is safe for viewing in the presence of family, co-workers, public audiences or in any other setting.
    Similar Worlds makes an extra effort to thoroughly screen (algorithmically) content entering this category, so that it is likely safe for even the youngest users of our website.
  • Non-Adult - This category is for content that is considered to be safe, free of Adult/Mature, Inappropriate, Abusive, Offensive or Sensitive content.
    This is typically content that is safe for viewing in most settings. Content falling in this category must not be obviously Mature or Inappropriate in nature.
    As Similar Worlds allows for freedom of speech, opinion and expression, we attempt to strike some balance as to not over-aggressively filter everything appearing in this category.
  • Mildly Adult - This category is for content which may be of "Mildly Adult" nature.
    This includes very slightly/mildly Adult/Mature, very slightly/mildly Inappropriate, very slightly/mildly Offensive or very slightly/mildly Sensitive content.
    Example: Mildly Adult Language, Jokes, References, Imagery.
    Content within this category may be considered as "PG 13" by today's standards, but not at/above "18+" / "R" rating.
  • Adult - This category is for content which may be of "Adult" nature.
    This includes Adult/Mature, Inappropriate, Offensive or Sensitive content.
    Example: Adult Language, Jokes, References, Imagery (not including nudity).
    Content within this category is not suitable for minors.
    Please note that we do not allow the posting of Nudity, Extremely Offensive or Abusive content, within areas of SW that are labelled as "Adult".
