  3. A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief that the material was disabled as a result of mistake or misidentification of the material to be disabled;

Cloudinary may decide, at its sole discretion that a sufficient reason exists for the immediate termination of your account for any reason, at any time. In these case Cloudinary may terminate your account immediately. Cloudinary may notify you that it canceled your account by sending a message to the e-mail address that you provided during the registration process. Such notification will come into effect immediately.
