COOKIES AND SIMILAR TECHNOLOGIES

When you access or use the Site, or interact with our Services, we may use “cookies” (or similar technologies), which store certain information on your device (i.e., locally stored) and which will allow us to customize the Service and content as well as enhance your experience of the Services. The use of cookies is a standard industry-wide practice. A "cookie" is a small piece of information that a website or assigns and stores on your device while you are viewing a website. Cookies are very helpful and can be used for various different purposes such as allowing you to navigate between pages efficiently and making the interaction between you and our Services quicker and easier, as well as to help customize your experience and for advertising purposes (including personalized advertising). You can find more information about cookies at www.allaboutcookies.org.

For more information about cookies and your controls in this regard, please review our Cookies Policy.

In addition, every user that interacts with our Platform will be assigned with a unique ID ("Unified ID"). The unique ID will enable us to customize the Service and content as well as enhance your experience. We will use the services of several companies such as The Trade Desk, ID5 and LiveRamp in order to generate the Unified ID. For more information about cookies and your controls in this regard, please review the Trade Desk Privacy policy available at: https://www.thetradedesk.com/general/privacy , ID5 Privacy policy available at https://id5.io/platform-privacy-policy and LiveRamp Privacy policy available at https://liveramp.com/privacy .
