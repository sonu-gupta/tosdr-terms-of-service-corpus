HOW TO ENTER:

No purchase or payment is necessary to participate in a Contest. Enter a Contest by completing each of the following steps:

  1. Download or open the HQ Application downloaded through the Apple Store on your mobile device, and either (a) if you already have a HQ account, log into your existing HQ account, or (b) if you do not already have a HQ account, sign up for a HQ account with a verified phone number.

  2. Click "Join." HQ will then automatically match you with other players. You will receive a notification when the live trivia game show match begins or when the live word puzzle match begins. In trivia matches, you will have 10 seconds to answer each question. In word puzzle matches, you will have 10 to 30 seconds to answer each puzzle, depending on the puzzle's difficulty. If you get the answer right, you will move on to the next round. If you get the answer wrong, you are eliminated for the remainder of the game.

  3. Play at least one HQ live trivia game show match or HQ live word puzzle match to completion.

SPINNING THE LETTER WHEEL

In some Contests, entrants are given the opportunity to spin a wheel (the “Wheel”). For instance, in HQ Words, entrants may be permitted to spin the Wheel to earn a letter, which will be automatically be revealed for that entrant in each puzzle in that Contest. An entrant may choose to spin the Wheel by following the in-App prompts. Spinning the Wheel is not required to participate in the Contest.

SPINNING THE SUPER WHEEL / USING A SUPER WHEEL An entrant participating in a Contest who has earned, been awarded, or purchased a Super Spin (“Super Spin” or "Super Wheel")may use that Super Spin to earn a pack of letters, which will be automatically be revealed for that entrant in each puzzle, or an individual letter (which will be automatically be revealed for that entrant in each puzzle) and an Extra Life (see Extra Life section below). By default, an entrant’s Super Spin will be used at the start of the HQ Words Contest unless the entrant chooses not to use it by following the in-App prompts. Each entrant can only use one Super Spin per game. Neither Super Spins (also known as Super Wheels) nor any other purchase or payment are required to participate in the Contest.

EXTRA LIFE

An entrant participating in a Contest who has earned, been awarded, or purchased an extra life (“Extra Life”) may use that Extra Life to reenter a Contest after being eliminated. By default, an entrant’s Extra Life will be used upon elimination unless the entrant chooses not to use it by following the in-App prompts. Each entrant can only use one Extra Life per game. Neither Extra Lives nor any other purchase or payment are required to participate in the Contest.

ERASER

An entrant participating in a Contest who has earned, been awarded, or purchased an eraser (“Eraser”) may use that Eraser to automatically remove one incorrect answer choice from a question. An entrant may choose to use an Eraser by following the in-App prompts. Each entrant can only use one Eraser per game. Erasers are not required to participate in the Contest.

POINT MULTIPLIER

An entrant participating in a Contest who has earned, been awarded, or purchased a Point Multiplier (“Point Multiplier”) may use that Point Multiplier to automatically multiply the number of points the entrant earns during a particular game. Each entrant can only use one Point Multiplier per game. Point Multipliers are not required to participate in the Contest.

_HQ Trivia_

In HQ Trivia contests, Players must select the correct answer to each question. If a player submits an incorrect answer or fails to select an answer in the allotted time, the player will be eliminated unless the player uses an Extra Life and has not yet used an Extra Life in that Contest. The winner of an HQ Trivia Contest will be the last person remaining at the end of each game show match. If there are multiple players remaining after the final question, such remaining players will split the Prize evenly. Sponsor may also offer “Winner Take All" HQ Trivia Contests. In "Winner Take All" HQ Trivia Contests, which Sponsor shall identify at the outset of the game show in which the Contest is offered, players will continue to receive questions until only a single player can correctly answer the question given. Sponsor will then award the Prize to the single remaining player. If all remaining players fail to answer a question correctly, resulting in every player's elimination, the Sponsor can decide to either roll the Prize for “Winner Take All" Contest over to a future game, which will be announced live at the end of the game, or bring all Players that answered the final question incorrectly back into the Contest.

Sponsor may also offer “Top N" HQ Trivia Contests. In “Top N" HQ Trivia Contests, which Sponsor shall identify at the outset of the game show in which the Contest is offered, players will continue to receive questions until N or fewer players can correctly answer the question given. Sponsor will then award the Prize to the single remaining player. If all remaining players fail to answer a question correctly, resulting in every player's elimination, the Sponsor can decide to either roll the Prize for “Winner Take All" Contest over to a future game, which will be announced live at the end of the game, or bring all Players that answered the final question incorrectly back into the Contest.

_HQ Words_

In HQ Words contests, Players select letters to solve word puzzles. If a player fails to solve a word puzzle in the allotted time or selects three incorrect letters, the player will be eliminated unless the player uses an Extra Life and has not yet used an Extra Life in that Contest. The winner of the Contest will be the last player remaining at the end of each match. If there are multiple players remaining after the final round, the Sponsor may split the Prize evenly among the remaining players, or the three players who solved the puzzles in the shortest amount of aggregate time will receive a pre-determined portion of the Prize of the Prize and all other players remaining after the final round will split the remainder of the Prize evenly.
