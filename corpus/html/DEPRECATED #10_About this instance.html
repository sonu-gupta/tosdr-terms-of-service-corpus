Welcome on mstdn.io! 🎉

Fast, secure and up-to-date instance, welcoming everyone around the world. Join us! 🌍 Up since 04/04/2017. ✅

Why should you sign up on mstdn.io?
  • This instance is not focused on any theme or subject, feel free to talk about whatever you want.
  • Although the main language is english, we accept every single language and country.
  • We're connected to the whole ActivityPub fediverse and we do not block any foreign instance nor user.
  • We do have rules, but all we want is to have responsible users.
  • The instance uses a powerful server to ensure speed and stability, and it has good uptime. We follow state-of-the-art security practices.
  • We have over 300 custom emojis to unleash your meme potential!

📜 Rules:

• Respect each other 🤝
• If you follow the Human Rights (EN/FR/JP), nothing bad should happen 😉
• Don't spam ✋
• No racism, xenophobia, sexism, discrimination, nazis... ☮️
• Please don't post illegal content (or links to) in France and Germany (except torrents) ⛔

If you're not sure about the rules whether it's because you want to sign up or post something, please contact the admin.

Due to spam, you'll have to apply before signing up. Please use real reasons for you application, otherwise you may be discarded as spam.


💻 What's behind?

This instance is hosted on a VM at Hetzner Cloud, in Germany.

You can see the status page to check our uptime.

Since we have users from all around the world (From Brasil to Japan, including Wester Europe), we use Cloudflare as a CDN among other things.

FYI, 30 days of the web server's logs are kept. Daily backups are made.
