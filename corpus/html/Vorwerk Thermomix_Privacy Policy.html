9 Your Rights (if you are an EU resident)

9.1 Right of Access

You have the right to ask if the Vorwerk Group Companies processes your personal data and, if so, that Vorwerk, LLC provides you with access to, and information on, the data stored by Vorwerk, LLC with regard to your person and your pseudonym.

9.2 Right of Correction

You have the right to correct your personal data if the personal data collected is inaccurate.

9.3 Right of Deletion

9.3.1 Under certain circumstances, you have the right to request the deletion of your personal data without undue delay. Such circumstances may be, for example, if personal data is no longer necessary for the purpose(s) for which it was collected or if you withdraw the consent on which our processing is based and if there is no other legal basis for the processing.

9.3.2 You may completely delete your user account on the Platform. To this end, it is sufficient to send an e-mail indicating in the subject line “delete my profile” to service@thermomix.us. The use of Cookidoo® and the App will no longer be possible once the user account has been deleted.

9.3.3 You may also delete the following data:

• The data recorded on the Thermomix® may be deleted using the reset function in the Thermomix® settings menu

• The network settings on your Thermomix® connected to the internet by means of the restore function to the initial state of the network in the “settings” section of the Thermomix® menu

• Recipes stored on the Thermomix® or the Cook-Key®, by using the reset function in the “settings” section of the Thermomix® menu

9.4 Right to Restrict Processing

You have the right to obtain a restriction of processing under certain circumstances. Such circumstance may be e.g. when you think that your personal data is not accurate or if we do not need the personal data for the original purpose of the processing but you need us to keep such data for the establishment, exercise or defense of legal claims.

9.5 Right to Data Portability

Under certain circumstances, you have the right to receive the personal data you provided to us, in a structure, commonly used and machine-readable format, and have the right to transmit that information to another data controller without hindrance or to ask us to do so.

9.6 Right to Object to Processing

You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data under certain circumstances, in particular if we process your personal data on the legal basis of legitimate interests or if we use your personal data for marketing purposes.

9.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

9.7.1 Where we process your personal data on the basis of consent given to us, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time. Please note that withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of consent-based processing prior to its withdrawal.

9.7.2 For specific instructions on how to withdraw consent, please also consult the

• section 4, in particular sections 4.4.7 and 4.5.8 concerning the tracking of use on the Platform and on your Thermomix®

• Section 7.4 which indicates how to unsubscribe from marketing emails

• section 8.3.4 concerning the synchronization of customer data between Vorwerk Group Companies

9.6 How to Exercise Data Protection Rights

For questions regarding the collection, processing, use, disclosure, correction, blocking, deletion, or objection to a specific type of processing of the data or the right to data portability, contact service@thermomix.us.

12 Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager

Cookidoo® and the App use Google Analytics and the Google Tag Manager, which are both web analysis services provided by Google Inc., 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA (“Google”). The information generated by the cookie regarding your use of Cookidoo® and the App is usually transferred to a Google server in the USA and is stored there.

You may prevent collection by Google of the data generated by the cookie and relating to your use of Cookidoo® and the App (including your IP address), as well as processing of this data by Google, by downloading and installing the browser plug-in provided at the following link: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout. As an alternative to the browser plugin or within browsers on mobile devices, you may click this link in order to opt-out from being tracked by Google Analytics and the Google Tag Manager within Cookidoo® in the future (the opt-out applies only for the browser in which you set it and within this domain): https://cookidoo.thermomix.com/vorwerkWebapp/app#/profile/my-privacy. An opt-out cookie will be stored on your device, which means that you will have to click this link again if you delete your cookies.
