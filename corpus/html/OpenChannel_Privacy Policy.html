Cookies

When you visit our website, we place a text file called a “cookie” in the browser directory of your computer’s hard drive. . A cookie is a small piece of information that a website can store on your web browser and later retrieve. . The cookie cannot be read by any website other than the one that set up the cookies. . Some browsers can be set to reject all cookies. . If you choose to modify your browser in this manner, your experience on this website will not be affected.

We also use cookies and work with third party service providers who use cookies to collect traffic data and other information about your use of our Website and Services.  We and our service providers use this information in aggregated and anonymous form to analyze usage of our Website and to improve the operation of our Website, Services and business generally.
