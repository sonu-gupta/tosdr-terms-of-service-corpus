ABCya.com Mobile Applications Privacy Policy
ABCya.com LLC (“ABCya”) mobile applications(the “Apps”) are teacher-created and intended to provide fun and educational games and apps for kids to use under the guidance of their parents and teachers. This Privacy Statement explains our privacy practices with regard to the Apps.

We Do Not Collect Any Information When You Download Or Use Our Applications.
When you download and use our mobile applications, we don’t require you to provide any information and we don’t collect any information from you or about you, your device, or your use of our mobile applications. In addition:

  • The Apps do not contain any third-party ads;
  • The Apps do not include any In App Purchases;
  • The Apps do not contain analytical or any other data collection tools. and
  • The Apps do not contain Social Network Integration with the exception of "COIN by ABCya.com" which includes integration with Apple's Game Center where available. If you are logged into your Game Center account, the COIN by ABCya.com App will share certain information regarding your use of the App, such as your score and username.
  • ABCya.com does not control Game Center and your use of Game Center is governed by Apple's policies.

Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may amend this Privacy Policy from time to time. Use of information we collect is subject to the Privacy Policy in effect at the time such information is collected unless you consent to additional use.

Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please send an email to: support@abcya.com
